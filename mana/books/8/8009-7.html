箴言．传道书．雅歌生命读经
　
中文标语
　
箴言．传道书．雅歌生命读经
　
一九九五年七月夏季训练
一个爱基督的人，应当是受祂的爱并祂的甜美所吸引，追求祂以得完全满足的人。
一个爱基督的人，应当是蒙祂呼召，借着与祂的十字架成为一，得拯救脱离己的人。
一个爱基督的人，当是蒙祂呼召，在升天里生活，在祂的复活里作神的新造。
Chinese New Year's Conference, February 1994
一个爱基督的人，应当是蒙祂更厉害的呼召，在经历祂的复活之后，借着祂的十字架，在幔内过生活的人。
　
英文标语
　
Life－Study of Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and Song of Songs
　
Summer Training, July 1995
A lover of Christ should be one who is attracted by His love and drawn by Him in His sweetness to pursue after Him for full satisfaction.
A lover of Christ should be one who is called by Him to be delivered from the self through his oneness with the cross of Christ.
A lover of Christ should be one who is called by Him to live in ascension as the new creation of God in the resurrection of Christ.
A lover of Christ should be one who is called by Him more strongly to live within the veil through His cross after the lover's experience of His resurrection.
