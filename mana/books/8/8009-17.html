罗马书中神完整的救恩结晶读经
　
中文标语
　
罗马书中神完整的救恩结晶读经
　
一九九六年十二月冬季训练
神完整的救恩是本于祂的义，并借着我们的信。
凭两个神圣的迁移：从亚当里迁到基督里，并从肉体里迁到那灵里。
在一个属灵的联结里：生命的灵与我们的灵，联结成为一个调和的灵。
使我们借着洋益之恩并洋溢之义的恩赐，在生命中作王。
　
英文标语
　
Crystallization－Study of the Complete Salvation of God in Romans
　
Winter Training, December 1996
God's complete salvation is based upon His righteousness and through our faith.
By the two divine transfers: out of Adam into Christ and out of the flesh into the Spirit.
In the one spiritual union of the Spirit of life with our spirit, forming a mingled spirit.
For us to reign in life by the abundance of grace and of the gift of righteousness.
