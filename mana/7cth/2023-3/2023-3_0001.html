二〇二三年国殇节国际特会

总题：腓立比书中所启示的认识、经历并享受基督

标　语

我们要以基督作一切并在一切事上得着祂，就必须学习一直否认己并转向灵。

In order to take Christ as everything and gain Him in all things, we must learn to continually deny our self and turn to our spirit.

我们需要借着思念同一件事，在基督的心肠里与祂是一，并让神为着祂的美意在我们里面运行，使我们立志并行事，而同魂，以享受基督。

We need to be one in soul to enjoy Christ by thinking the one thing, by being one with Christ in His inward parts, and by allowing God to operate in us the willing and the working for His good pleasure.

我们应当像保罗一样，渴望认识并经历基督、祂复活的大能以及同祂受苦的交通，并以基督的死作为基督徒生活的模子而模成祂的死。

Like Paul, we should aspire to know and experience Christ, the power of His resurrection, and the fellowship of His sufferings and to be conformed to His death as the mold of the Christian life.

我们需要模成基督的死，好使我们可以达到那从死人中杰出的复活；这是主能在祂的恢复里往前唯一的路，是主建造祂召会唯一的路，是新妇将自己预备好唯一的路，也是主回来唯一的路。

We need to be conformed to Christ's death so that we may attain to the out～resurrection— the only way for the Lord to go on in His recovery, the only way to build up His church, the only way for the bride to make herself ready, and the only way for the Lord to come back.
