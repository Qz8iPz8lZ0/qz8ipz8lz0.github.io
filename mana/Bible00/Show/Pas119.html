诗篇-Psalms 119

Settings

  • 119:1　Blessed are those whose way is perfect, Who walk in the law of Jehovah.
  • 行径完全，遵行耶和华律法的，这样的人是有福的。
  • 119:2　Blessed are those who keep His testimonies, Who seek Him with all their heart.
  • 遵守祂的法度，全心寻求祂的，这样的人是有福的。
  • 119:3　Surely they do no wrong; They walk in His ways.
  • 他们不作不义的事，惟遵行祂的道路。
  • 119:4　You have commanded that Your precepts Be diligently kept.
  • 耶和华阿，你曾将你的训辞吩咐我们，要我们殷勤遵守。
  • 119:5　Oh that my ways may be established So as to keep Your statutes!
  • 但愿我的行径坚定，得以遵守你的律例！
  • 119:6　Then I will not be put to shame, When I regard all Your commandments.
  • 我看重你的一切诫命，就不至于羞愧。
  • 119:7　I will give thanks to You with uprightness of heart When I learn Your righteous ordinances.
  • 我学了你公义的典章，就以正直的心称谢你。
  • 119:8　I will keep Your statutes; Do not utterly forsake me.
  • 我必守你的律例；求你总不要丢弃我。
  • 119:9　With what should a young man keep his way pure? By guarding it according to Your word.
  • 少年人用什么使他的行径纯洁呢？是要遵行你的话。
  • 119:10　With all my heart I have sought You; Do not let me wander from Your commandments.
  • 我全心寻求了你，求你不要叫我偏离你的诫命。
  • 119:11　In my heart I have treasured up Your word That I might not sin against You.
  • 我将你的话珍藏在心里，免得我得罪你。
  • 119:12　Blessed are You, O Jehovah; Teach me Your statutes.
  • 耶和华阿，你是当受颂赞的；求你将你的律例教训我。
  • 119:13　With my lips I have declared All the ordinances of Your mouth.
  • 我用嘴唇述说你口中的一切典章。
  • 119:14　I have rejoiced in the way of Your testimonies As much as in all riches.
  • 我喜悦你法度的道路，如同喜悦一切的财物。
  • 119:15　I will muse upon Your precepts And regard Your ways.
  • 我要默想你的训辞，看重你的道路。
  • 119:16　I will take delight in Your statutes; I will not forget Your word.
  • 我要在你的律例中自乐；我不忘记你的话。
  • 119:17　Deal bountifully with Your servant that I may live And keep Your word.
  • 求你厚待你的仆人，使我存活，并且遵守你的话。
  • 119:18　Open my eyes that I may behold Wondrous things out of Your law.
  • 求你开我的眼睛，使我看出你律法中的奇妙。
  • 119:19　I am a sojourner in the earth; Do not hide Your commandments from me.
  • 我是在地上寄居的；求你不要向我隐瞒你的诫命。
  • 119:20　My soul is crushed with longing For Your ordinances at all times.
  • 我时刻羡慕你的典章，甚至心碎。
  • 119:21　You have rebuked the proud, the cursed, Who wander from Your commandments.
  • 偏离你诫命，受咒诅的骄傲人，你已经责备他们。
  • 119:22　Roll away from me reproach and contempt, For I have kept Your testimonies.
  • 求你除掉我所受的羞辱和藐视，因我遵守你的法度。
  • 119:23　Princes as well have sat and spoken against me, But Your servant mused upon Your statutes.
  • 虽有首领坐着妄论我，你仆人却默想你的律例。
  • 119:24　Also Your testimonies are my delight; They are my counselors.
  • 你的法度是我所喜乐的，是我的谋士。
  • 119:25　My soul clings to the dust; Enliven me according to Your word.
  • 我的性命几乎归于尘土；求你照你的话将我救活。
  • 119:26　I have recounted my ways, and You have answered me; Teach me Your statutes.
  • 我述说我的行径，你应允了我；求你将你的律例教训我。
  • 119:27　Cause me to understand the way of Your precepts, And I will muse upon Your wondrous deeds.
  • 求你使我明白你训辞的道路，我就默想你的奇事。
  • 119:28　My soul melts because of grief; Strengthen me according to Your word.
  • 我的心因愁苦而融化；求你照你的话使我坚立。
  • 119:29　Remove from me the way of falsehood, And graciously grant me Your law.
  • 求你使我离开虚谎的道路，开恩将你的律法赐给我。
  • 119:30　I have chosen the way of faithfulness; Your ordinances I have set before me.
  • 我拣选了忠信的道路，将你的典章摆在我面前。
  • 119:31　I cling to Your testimonies; O Jehovah, do not put me to shame.
  • 我紧紧跟随你的法度；耶和华阿，求你不要叫我羞愧。
  • 119:32　I run the way of Your commandments, For You enlarge my heart.
  • 你既开广我的心，我就奔跑你诫命的道路。
  • 119:33　Teach me, O Jehovah, the way of Your statutes; And I will keep it to the end.
  • 耶和华阿，求你将你律例的道路指教我，我必遵守到底。
  • 119:34　Give me understanding, and I will observe Your law; Indeed I will keep it with all my heart.
  • 求你赐我悟性，我便遵守你的律法；且要全心谨守。
  • 119:35　Cause me to go on the path of Your commandments, For in it I delight.
  • 求你使我行在你诫命的道路上，因为我喜悦这道路。
  • 119:36　Incline my heart to Your testimonies And not to unjust gain.
  • 求你使我的心趋向你的法度，不趋向不义之财。
  • 119:37　Turn my eyes from beholding vanity, And enliven me in Your ways.
  • 求你叫我转眼不看虚假，叫我在你的道路中活着。
  • 119:38　Confirm to Your servant Your word, Which is for fearing You.
  • 求你坚定你对仆人的话，这话是叫人敬畏你。
  • 119:39　Turn away my reproach, which I fear; For Your ordinances are good.
  • 求你使我所怕的羞辱远离我；因你的典章本为美。
  • 119:40　Behold, I have longed after Your precepts; Enliven me in Your righteousness.
  • 看哪，我羡慕你的训辞；求你使我在你的公义中活着。
  • 119:41　May Your lovingkindness also come to me, O Jehovah, Your salvation according to Your word.
  • 耶和华阿，愿你照你的话，使你的慈爱，就是你的救恩，临到我身上。
  • 119:42　Thus I will have an answer for him who reproaches me, For I trust in Your word.
  • 我就有话回答那羞辱我的，因我信靠你的话。
  • 119:43　And do not take the word of truth utterly from my mouth, For I have hoped in Your ordinances.
  • 求你叫真理的话，总不离开我口，因我仰望了你的典章。
  • 119:44　Thus I will keep Your law continually, Forever and ever.
  • 我要常守你的律法，直到永永远远。
  • 119:45　And I will walk at liberty, For I have sought Your precepts.
  • 我要行在宽阔之地，因我寻求了你的训辞。
  • 119:46　I will also speak of Your testimonies before kings And not be ashamed.
  • 我也要在君王面前，论说你的法度，并不以为耻。
  • 119:47　And I will take delight in Your commandments, Which I love.
  • 我要在你的诫命中自乐，这些诫命是我所爱的。
  • 119:48　And I will lift up my hand to Your commandments, which I love; And I will muse upon Your statutes.
  • 我要向你的诫命举手，这些诫命是我所爱的；我也要默想你的律例。
  • 119:49　Remember the word to Your servant In which You have made me hope.
  • 求你记念向你仆人所说的话，你曾使我在这话中有盼望。
  • 119:50　This is my comfort in my affliction, For Your word has enlivened me.
  • 这是我在患难中的安慰，因为你的话将我救活。
  • 119:51　The proud have derided me greatly; I have not swerved from Your law.
  • 骄傲的人极力讥诮我；我却没有偏离你的律法。
  • 119:52　I have remembered Your ordinances from long ago, O Jehovah, and have comforted myself.
  • 耶和华阿，我记念你从古以来的典章，就得了安慰。
  • 119:53　Raging indignation seizes me because of the wicked, Who forsake Your law.
  • 我因恶人离弃你的律法，就怒气发作，犹如火烧。
  • 119:54　Your statutes have become my songs of praise In the house of my pilgrimage.
  • 在我寄居的住所，你的律例成了我的诗歌。
  • 119:55　I have remembered Your name In the night, O Jehovah, And have kept Your law.
  • 耶和华阿，我夜间记念你的名，遵守你的律法。
  • 119:56　This has been mine, That I have kept Your precepts.
  • 我所以如此，是因我守了你的训辞。
  • 119:57　Jehovah is my portion; I have promised to keep Your words.
  • 耶和华是我的业分；我曾说，我要遵守你的言语。
  • 119:58　I entreated Your favor with my whole heart; Be gracious to me according to Your word.
  • 我全心求过你的恩；愿你照你的话恩待我。
  • 119:59　I considered my ways And turned my feet toward Your testimonies.
  • 我筹算自己的道路，就转步归向你的法度。
  • 119:60　I hurried and did not delay To keep Your commandments.
  • 我急忙遵守你的诫命，并不迟延。
  • 119:61　The cords of the wicked surround me, But I have not forgotten Your law.
  • 恶人的绳索缠绕我，我却没有忘记你的律法。
  • 119:62　At midnight I rise up to give thanks to You Because of Your righteous ordinances.
  • 我因你公义的典章，半夜起来称谢你。
  • 119:63　I am a companion to all who fear You And to those who keep Your precepts.
  • 凡敬畏你，守你训辞的人，我都与他作伴。
  • 119:64　Your lovingkindness, O Jehovah, fills the earth; Teach me Your statutes.
  • 耶和华阿，你的慈爱遍满大地；求你将你的律例教训我。
  • 119:65　You have dealt well with Your servant, O Jehovah, according to Your word.
  • 耶和华阿，你向来是照你的话善待仆人。
  • 119:66　Teach me proper discernment and knowledge, For I believe in Your commandments.
  • 求你将明辨和知识教训我，因我相信你的诫命。
  • 119:67　Before I was afflicted, I went astray; But now I keep Your word.
  • 我受苦以先，走迷了路；现在却遵守你的话。
  • 119:68　You are good and One who does good; Teach me Your statutes.
  • 你本为善，所行的也善；求你将你的律例教训我。
  • 119:69　The proud have plastered lies against me; With my whole heart I will keep Your precepts.
  • 骄傲人编造谎言攻击我；我却要全心守你的训辞。
  • 119:70　Their heart is heavy as with fat; But I delight in Your law.
  • 他们的心迟钝，如蒙脂油；我却以你的律法为乐。
  • 119:71　It is good for me that I have been afflicted, That I might learn Your statutes.
  • 我受苦是与我有益，为要使我学习你的律例。
  • 119:72　The law of Your mouth is better to me Than thousands of pieces of gold and silver.
  • 你口中的律法与我有益，胜过千万的金银。
  • 119:73　Your hands have made me and fashioned me; Give me understanding that I may learn Your commandments.
  • 你的手造作我、建立我；求你赐我悟性，可以学习你的诫命。
  • 119:74　Those who fear You will see me and rejoice, For I have hoped in Your word.
  • 敬畏你的人看见我就喜乐，因我仰望你的话。
  • 119:75　I know, O Jehovah, that Your judgments are righteous And that in faithfulness You afflicted me.
  • 耶和华阿，我知道你的判语是公义的；你使我受苦，是以信实待我。
  • 119:76　Let Your lovingkindness, I beseech You, be my comfort, According to Your word to Your servant.
  • 求你照着你对仆人所说的话，以慈爱安慰我。
  • 119:77　Let Your compassions come to me, and I will live; For Your law is my delight.
  • 愿你的怜恤临到我，使我存活；因你的律法是我的喜乐。
  • 119:78　Let the proud be put to shame, for they have subverted my cause with a lie; But I will muse upon Your precepts.
  • 愿骄傲人蒙羞，因为他们以谎言屈枉我；但我要默想你的训辞。
  • 119:79　Let those who fear You turn to me, And they will know Your testimonies.
  • 愿敬畏你的人归向我，他们就知道你的法度。
  • 119:80　May my heart be perfect in Your statutes, That I may not be put to shame.
  • 愿我的心在你的律例上完全，使我不至蒙羞。
  • 119:81　My soul faints while longing for Your salvation; I hope in Your word.
  • 我的魂因渴想你的救恩以致衰竭；我仰望你的话语。
  • 119:82　My eyes faint while longing for Your word, As I say, When will You comfort me?
  • 我的眼因盼望你的话语以致昏蒙，我说，你几时才安慰我？
  • 119:83　For I have become like a wineskin in the smoke; But I do not forget Your statutes.
  • 我好像烟薰的皮袋，但我不忘记你的律例。
  • 119:84　How many are the days of Your servant? When will You execute justice upon my persecutors?
  • 你仆人的日子有多少？你几时才向逼迫我的人施行公理呢？
  • 119:85　The proud have dug pits for me, Those who are not according to Your law.
  • 不依从你律法的骄傲人，为我掘了坑。
  • 119:86　All Your commandments are faithful; They persecute me falsely; help me.
  • 你的诫命尽都信实；他们诡诈的逼迫我；求你帮助我。
  • 119:87　They have almost consumed me upon the earth; But I have not forsaken Your precepts.
  • 他们几乎把我从地上灭绝；但我没有离弃你的训辞。
  • 119:88　Enliven me according to Your lovingkindness, And I will keep the testimony of Your mouth.
  • 求你照你的慈爱将我救活，我就遵守你口中的法度。
  • 119:89　Forever, O Jehovah, Does Your word stand firm in heaven.
  • 耶和华阿，你的话立定在天，直到永远。
  • 119:90　Your faithfulness is from generation to generation; You established the earth, and it abides.
  • 你的信实存到万代；你坚定了地，地就长存。
  • 119:91　They abide to this day according to Your ordinances, For all things are Your servants.
  • 万物照你的判语存到今日，因为它们都是你的仆役。
  • 119:92　Unless Your law had been my delight, I would have perished in my affliction.
  • 我若不是以你的律法为乐，早就在患难中灭绝了。
  • 119:93　I will never forget Your precepts, For by them You have enlivened me.
  • 我永不忘记你的训辞，因你用这训辞将我救活。
  • 119:94　I am Yours; save me, For I have sought Your precepts.
  • 我是属你的；求你救我，因我寻求了你的训辞。
  • 119:95　The wicked have waited for me in order to destroy me, But I consider Your testimonies.
  • 恶人等待我，要灭绝我，我却要揣摩你的法度。
  • 119:96　I have seen an end to all perfection, But Your commandment is all-expansive.
  • 我看一切圆满的事都有尽头，惟有你的诫命极其宽广。
  • 119:97　Oh, how I love Your law! All day long it is my musing.
  • 我何等爱你的律法，终日不住的默想。
  • 119:98　Your commandments make me wiser than my enemies, For they are always with me.
  • 你的诫命使我比仇敌有智慧，因这些诫命常与我同在。
  • 119:99　I have more insight than all my teachers, For Your testimonies are my musing.
  • 我比我所有的师傅更通达，因我默想你的法度。
  • 119:100　I understand more than the aged, For I have kept Your precepts.
  • 我比年老的更明白，因我守了你的训辞。
  • 119:101　I have withheld my feet from every evil way, That I might keep Your word.
  • 我禁止我的脚走一切的邪路，为要遵守你的话。
  • 119:102　I have not turned aside from Your ordinances, For You have taught me.
  • 我没有偏离你的典章，因为你教训了我。
  • 119:103　How sweet are Your words to my taste! Sweeter than honey to my mouth!
  • 你的言语在我上膛何等甘美！在我口中比蜜更甜！
  • 119:104　Through Your precepts I gain understanding; Therefore I hate every false way.
  • 我借着你的训辞，得以明白；所以我恨一切虚假的行径。
  • 119:105　Your word is a lamp to my feet And a light to my path.
  • 你的话是我脚前的灯，是我路上的光。
  • 119:106　I have sworn and will confirm it, That I will keep Your righteous ordinances.
  • 我曾起誓遵守你公义的典章，我必坚守这誓言。
  • 119:107　I have been greatly afflicted; O Jehovah, enliven me according to Your word.
  • 我甚是受苦；耶和华阿，求你照你的话将我救活。
  • 119:108　Accept, I pray, the freewill offerings of my mouth, O Jehovah; And teach me Your ordinances.
  • 耶和华阿，求你悦纳我口中的甘心祭，且将你的典章教训我。
  • 119:109　My soul is continually in my hand, But I do not forget Your law.
  • 我的性命常在危险之中，我却不忘记你的律法。
  • 119:110　The wicked have laid a trap for me, But I do not stray from Your precepts.
  • 恶人为我设下网罗，我却没有偏离你的训辞而迷失。
  • 119:111　Your testimonies are my heritage forever, For they are the gladness of my heart.
  • 你的法度是我永远的产业，因为这是我心中的欢乐。
  • 119:112　I have inclined my heart to perform Your statutes Forever, to the end.
  • 我的心倾向你的律例，要永远遵行，一直到底。
  • 119:113　I hate those who are double-minded, But I love Your law.
  • 心怀二意的人，为我所恨；但你的律法，为我所爱。
  • 119:114　You are my hiding place and my shield; In Your word do I hope.
  • 你是我藏身之处，又是我的盾牌；我仰望你的话语。
  • 119:115　Depart from me, you evildoers, That I may keep the commandments of my God.
  • 你们作恶的人离开我罢，我好遵守我神的诫命。
  • 119:116　Uphold me according to Your word that I may live, And do not let me be ashamed of my hope.
  • 求你照你的话扶持我，使我存活，也不叫我因所盼望的蒙羞。
  • 119:117　Hold me up that I may be safe, And I will regard Your statutes continually.
  • 求你扶持我，使我得救，我必时常看重你的律例。
  • 119:118　You spurn all those who stray from Your statutes, For their deceitfulness is vain.
  • 凡偏离你律例的人，你都轻弃他们，因为他们的诡诈必归虚空。
  • 119:119　You do away with all the wicked ones of the earth like dross; Therefore I love Your testimonies.
  • 凡地上的恶人，你除掉他们好像除掉渣滓；因此我爱你的法度。
  • 119:120　My flesh trembles for fear of You, And I am afraid of Your judgments.
  • 我因惧怕你，肉就发抖，我也怕你的判语。
  • 119:121　I have performed justice and righteousness; Do not leave me to my oppressors.
  • 我行过公理和公义；求你不要把我撇给欺压我的人。
  • 119:122　Guarantee Your servant's well-being; Do not let the proud oppress me.
  • 求你为仆人作保，使我得好处；不容骄傲人欺压我。
  • 119:123　My eyes faint while longing for Your salvation And for Your righteous word.
  • 我的眼因盼望你的救恩和你公义的话，以致昏蒙。
  • 119:124　Deal with Your servant according to Your lovingkindness, And teach me Your statutes.
  • 求你照你的慈爱待仆人，将你的律例教训我。
  • 119:125　I am Your servant; give me understanding, That I may know Your testimonies.
  • 我是你的仆人；求你赐我悟性，使我得知你的法度。
  • 119:126　It is time for Jehovah to act, For they have broken Your law.
