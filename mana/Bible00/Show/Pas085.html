诗篇-Psalms 85

Settings

  • 85:0
  • 可拉子孙的诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 85:1　You have been pleased, O Jehovah, with Your land; You have turned the captivity of Jacob.
  • 耶和华阿，你喜悦了你的地，使雅各从被掳归回。
  • 85:2　You have forgiven the iniquity of Your people; You have covered all their sin.
  • 你赦免了你百姓的罪孽，遮盖了他们一切的罪。　细拉
  • 85:3　You have taken away all Your wrath; You have turned from the fierceness of Your anger.
  • 你收去了所发的忿怒，使你猛烈的怒气转消。
  • 85:4　Restore us, O God of our salvation, And cause Your indignation toward us to cease.
  • 拯救我们的神阿，求你恢复我们，止住你向我们所发的恼怒。
  • 85:5　Will You be angry with us forever? Will You extend Your anger from generation to generation?
  • 你要向我们发怒到永远么？你要将你的怒气延留到万代么？
  • 85:6　Will You not again revive us, That Your people may rejoice in You?
  • 你不再将我们救活，使你的百姓因你喜乐么？
  • 85:7　Show us, O Jehovah, Your lovingkindness, And grant us Your salvation.
  • 耶和华阿，求你向我们显示你的慈爱，又将你的救恩赐给我们。
  • 85:8　I will hear what God Jehovah will speak, For He will speak peace To His people and to His faithful ones; But let them not turn again unto folly.
  • 我要听神耶和华所说的话，因为祂必向祂的百姓，祂的虔诚人，说平安的话；他们却不可再转向愚妄。
  • 85:9　Surely His salvation is near to those who fear Him, That glory may dwell in our land.
  • 祂的救恩，诚然与敬畏祂的人相近，叫荣耀住在我们的地上。
  • 85:10　Lovingkindness and truth have met together; Righteousness and peace have kissed each other.
  • 慈爱和真实，彼此相遇；公义和平安，彼此相亲。
  • 85:11　Truth has sprung forth from the earth, And righteousness has looked down from heaven.
  • 真实从地生出，公义从天俯视。
  • 85:12　Indeed Jehovah will give what is good, And our land will give its increase.
  • 耶和华必赐下好处，我们的地要多出土产。
  • 85:13　Righteousness will go before Him And make His footsteps a way to walk in.
  • 公义要行在祂面前，叫祂的脚踪成为可走的路。
