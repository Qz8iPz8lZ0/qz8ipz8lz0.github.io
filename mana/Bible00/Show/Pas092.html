诗篇-Psalms 92

Settings

  • 92:0
  • 一首诗，安息日的歌。
  • 92:1　It is good to give thanks to Jehovah And to sing psalms to Your name, O Most High;
  • 称谢耶和华，歌颂你至高者的名，
  • 92:2　To declare Your lovingkindness in the morning And Your faithfulness in the nights,
  • 早晨传扬你的慈爱，夜间传扬你的信实，
  • 92:3　Upon an instrument of ten strings and upon a harp, Upon the higgaion of the lyre.
  • 弹奏十弦的乐器和瑟，用琴弹出幽雅的声音；这本为美事。
  • 92:4　For You have made me rejoice, O Jehovah, because of what You have done; Because of the works of Your hands I will shout for joy.
  • 因为耶和华阿，你借着你所作的使我喜乐；因你手的工作，我要欢呼。
  • 92:5　How great are Your works, O Jehovah! So very deep are Your thoughts!
  • 耶和华阿，你的工作何其大！你的意念极其深！
  • 92:6　A senseless man does not know them, Nor does a fool understand them.
  • 无知人不晓得，愚顽人也不明白。
  • 92:7　When the wicked sprout like grass, And all the workers of iniquity blossom, It is so that they may be destroyed forever.
  • 恶人茂盛如草，一切作孽之人发旺的时候，正是他们要灭亡，直到永远。
  • 92:8　But You are on high forever, O Jehovah.
  • 耶和华阿，惟有你为至高，直到永远。
  • 92:9　For behold, Your enemies, O Jehovah; For behold, Your enemies will perish; All the workers of iniquity will be scattered.
  • 耶和华，看哪，你的仇敌；看哪，你的仇敌都要灭亡；一切作孽的都要离散。
  • 92:10　But You have exalted my horn like that of a wild ox; I am anointed with fresh oil.
  • 你却高举了我的角，如野牛的角；我是被新油膏了的。
  • 92:11　And my eye has seen its desire for my enemies; My ears have heard their desire for the evildoers who rise up against me.
  • 我眼睛看见我的仇敌遭报；我耳朵听见那些起来攻击我的作恶之人受罚。
  • 92:12　The righteous man will flourish like the palm tree; He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.
  • 义人要发旺如棕树，生长如利巴嫩的香柏树。
  • 92:13　Planted in the house of Jehovah, They will flourish in the courts of our God.
  • 他们栽植于耶和华的殿中，发旺在我们神的院里。
  • 92:14　They will still bring forth fruit in old age; They will be full of sap and green;
  • 他们年老的时候，仍要结果子；要满了汁浆而常发青；
  • 92:15　To declare that Jehovah is upright; He is my rock, and there is no unrighteousness in Him.
  • 好显明耶和华是正直的；祂是我的磐石，在祂毫无不义。
