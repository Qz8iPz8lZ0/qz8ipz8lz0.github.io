诗篇-Psalms 110

Settings

  • 110:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 110:1　Jehovah declares to my Lord, Sit at My right hand Until I make Your enemies Your footstool.
  • 耶和华对我主说，你坐在我的右边，等我使你的仇敌作你的脚凳。
  • 110:2　Jehovah will send forth The scepter of Your strength from Zion: Rule in the midst of Your enemies.
  • 耶和华必从锡安伸出你能力的杖来：你要在你的仇敌中间掌权。
  • 110:3　Your people will offer themselves willingly In the day of Your warfare, In the splendor of their consecration. Your young men will be to You Like the dew from the womb of the dawn.
  • 当你争战的日子，你的民要以奉献为彩饰，甘心献上自己。你的少年人对你必如清晨的甘露。
  • 110:4　Jehovah has sworn, And He will not change: You are a Priest forever According to the order of Melchizedek.
  • 耶和华起了誓，必不后悔；祂说，你是照着麦基洗德的等次，永远为祭司。
  • 110:5　The Lord is at Your right hand; He will shatter kings in the day of His anger.
  • 主在你右边；祂发怒的日子，必打伤列王。
  • 110:6　He will execute judgment among the nations; He will fill the place with corpses; He will shatter the head Over a great land.
  • 祂要在列邦中施行审判，使那处满了尸首；祂要在大地上打伤仇敌的头。
  • 110:7　He will drink from the brook by the way; Therefore He will lift up His head.
  • 祂要喝路旁的河水；因此祂必抬起头来。
