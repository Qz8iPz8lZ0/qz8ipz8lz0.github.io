诗篇-Psalms 35

Settings

  • 35:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 35:1　Strive, O Jehovah, with those who strive with me; Battle against those who battle against me.
  • 耶和华阿，与我相争的，求你与他们相争；与我相战的，求你与他们相战。
  • 35:2　Take hold of buckler and shield, And rise up as my help.
  • 求你拿着大小的盾牌，起来帮助我；
  • 35:3　Draw out also the spear, and close up the way Against those who pursue me; Say to my soul, I am your salvation.
  • 抽出枪来，挡住那追赶我的；求你对我的魂说：我是你的拯救。
  • 35:4　Let those who seek my life Be put to shame and humiliated; Let those who devise evil for me Be turned back and confounded.
  • 愿那寻索我命的，蒙羞受辱；愿那谋害我的，退后羞愧。
  • 35:5　Let them be like chaff before the wind, With the angel of Jehovah driving them on.
  • 愿他们像风前的糠秕，有耶和华的使者赶逐他们。
  • 35:6　Let their way be darkness and slipperiness itself, With the angel of Jehovah pursuing them.
  • 愿他们的道路，又暗又滑，有耶和华的使者追赶他们。
  • 35:7　For without cause they hid their net for me; Without cause they dug a pit for me.
  • 因他们无故的为我暗设网罗；无故的挖坑要害我的命。
  • 35:8　Let destruction come upon him unawares, And let his net, which he hid, catch him; Let him fall into it, into destruction.
  • 愿毁灭不知不觉间临到他身上；愿他暗设的网缠住自己；愿他落在其中灭没。
  • 35:9　Then my soul will exult in Jehovah; It will rejoice in His salvation.
  • 我的魂就必因耶和华欢腾，因祂的救恩欢喜。
  • 35:10　All my bones will say, Jehovah, who is like You, Who delivers the poor from him who is too strong for him, And the poor and needy from him who robs him?
  • 我的骨头都要说，耶和华阿，谁能像你救护穷苦人，脱离那比他强壮的，救护困苦穷乏人，脱离那抢夺他的？
  • 35:11　Malicious witnesses rise up; They ask me about things I know nothing of.
  • 恶毒的见证人起来，盘问我所不知道的事。
  • 35:12　They repay me evil for good; I am bereaved in soul.
  • 他们向我以恶报善，使我的魂孤苦。
  • 35:13　But I, when they were sick, Had sackcloth as my clothing; I afflicted my soul with fasting, And my prayer returned to my own bosom.
  • 至于我，当他们有病的时候，我便穿麻衣，禁食刻苦己心；我的祷告回到自己的怀中。
  • 35:14　I conducted myself as if it had been my friend or my brother; Like one mourning for his mother, I bowed down gloomily.
  • 我这样行，好像他是我的朋友，我的弟兄；我悲哀屈身，如同人为母亲哀痛。
  • 35:15　But at my stumbling they rejoice and gather together; The attackers, whom I did not know Gather together against me; They tear at me and do not cease.
  • 但我跌倒了，他们却欢喜，大家聚集；我所不认识的那些毁谤者，聚集攻击我；他们不住的把我撕裂。
  • 35:16　As profane mocking parasites, They gnash their teeth at me.
  • 他们如同不虔敬、好讥诮的阿谀之人，向我咬牙切齿。
  • 35:17　O Lord, how long will You look on? Rescue my soul from their destruction, My precious life from the lions.
  • 主阿，你看着不理，要到几时呢？求你救我的魂脱离他们的残害，救我宝贵的生命脱离少壮狮子。
  • 35:18　I will give You thanks in the great congregation; Among a mighty people I will praise You.
  • 我要在大会中称谢你，在强大的民中赞美你。
  • 35:19　Do not let those who are wrongly my enemies rejoice over me; Do not let those who hate me without cause narrow their eyes at me.
  • 求你不让那无理与我为仇的，向我夸耀；不让那无故恨我的，向我挤眼。
  • 35:20　For they do not speak peace, But devise words of deceit Against the quiet people of the land.
  • 因为他们不说和平话，倒设想诡诈的言语，害地上的安静人。
  • 35:21　And they open their mouth wide against me; They say, Aha! Aha! Our eyes have seen it.
  • 他们大大张口攻击我，说，阿哈！阿哈！我们的眼已经看见了。
  • 35:22　You have seen, O Jehovah; do not be silent. O Lord, do not be far from me.
  • 耶和华阿，你已经看见了；求你不要闭口。主阿，求你不要远离我。
  • 35:23　Awake, and stir Yourself for my cause, For my claim, O my God and my Lord.
  • 我的神我的主阿，求你奋兴醒起，判清我的事，伸明我的冤。
  • 35:24　Vindicate me according to Your righteousness, O Jehovah my God; And do not let them rejoice over me.
  • 耶和华我的神阿，求你按你的公义为我伸冤，不让他们向我夸耀。
  • 35:25　Do not let them say in their heart, Aha! That is what we wanted. Do not let them say, We have swallowed him up.
  • 不让他们心里说，阿哈！遂我们的心愿了；不让他们说，我们已经把他吞了。
  • 35:26　May those who rejoice at my trouble Be ashamed and confounded together; May those who magnify themselves against me Be clothed with shame and humiliation.
  • 愿那喜欢我遭难的，一同抱愧蒙羞；愿那向我妄自尊大的，披惭愧，蒙羞辱。
  • 35:27　May those who delight in the vindication of my righteousness Give a ringing shout and rejoice, And may they say continually, Jehovah be magnified, Who delights in the well-being of His servant.
  • 愿那喜悦我公义得表白的，欢呼喜乐；愿他们常说，当尊耶和华为大，祂喜悦祂的仆人安好。
  • 35:28　And my tongue will declare Your righteousness And Your praise all day long.
  • 我的舌头要终日论说你的公义，并且赞美你。
