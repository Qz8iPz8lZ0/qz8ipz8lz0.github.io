诗篇-Psalms 24

Settings

  • 24:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 24:1　The earth is Jehovah's, and its fullness, The habitable land and those who dwell in it.
  • 地和其中所充满的，世界和住在其间的，都属耶和华。
  • 24:2　For it is He who founded it upon the seas And established it upon the streams.
  • 祂把地奠定在海上，坚立在江河之上。
  • 24:3　Who may ascend the mountain of Jehovah, And who may stand in His holy place?
  • 谁能登耶和华的山，谁能站在祂的圣所？
  • 24:4　He who has clean hands and a pure heart, Who has not lifted up his soul to falsehood Or sworn deceitfully.
  • 就是手洁心清，魂不仰慕虚妄，不诡诈起誓的人。
  • 24:5　He will receive blessing from Jehovah, And righteousness from the God of his salvation.
  • 他必从耶和华得着祝福，又从救他的神得着公义。
  • 24:6　This is the generation of those who seek Him, Those who seek Your face, even Jacob.
  • 这是寻求耶和华的族类，是寻求你面的雅各。　细拉
  • 24:7　Lift up your heads, O gates; And be lifted up, O long enduring doors; And the King of glory will come in.
  • 众城门哪，你们要抬起头来；永久的门户阿，你们要被举起；荣耀的王将要进来。
  • 24:8　Who is the King of glory? Jehovah strong and mighty! Jehovah mighty in battle!
  • 那荣耀的王是谁呢？就是刚强大能的耶和华，在争战中有大能的耶和华！
  • 24:9　Lift up your heads, O gates; And lift up, O long enduring doors; And the King of glory will come in.
  • 众城门哪，你们要抬起头来；永久的门户阿，你们要把头抬起；荣耀的王将要进来。
  • 24:10　Who is this King of glory? Jehovah of hosts-He is the King of glory!
  • 那荣耀的王是谁呢？万军之耶和华－祂是荣耀的王！　细拉
