诗篇-Psalms 4

Settings

  • 4:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长；用丝弦的乐器。
  • 4:1　When I call out, answer me, O God of my righteousness; Make room for me when I am in straits; Be gracious to me and hear my prayer.
  • 显我为义的神阿，我呼求的时候，求你应允我；我在窘困中，求你使我宽广；求你恩待我，听我的祷告。
  • 4:2　O sons of men, how long will my glory be made a reproach? How long will you love vanity, will you seek a lie?
  • 人哪，你们将我的荣耀变为羞辱，要到几时呢？你们喜爱虚妄，寻求虚谎，要到几时呢？　细拉
  • 4:3　But know that Jehovah sets apart the faithful man for Himself. Jehovah hears when I call out to Him.
  • 你们要知道，耶和华已经分别虔诚人归祂自己；我呼求耶和华，祂必听我。
  • 4:4　Be angry, yet do not sin; Consider in your heart upon your bed, And be silent.
  • 你们生气，却不要犯罪；在床上的时候，要心里思想，并要肃静。　细拉
  • 4:5　Offer sacrifices of righteousness, And trust in Jehovah.
  • 当献上公义的祭，又当信靠耶和华。
  • 4:6　Many are those who say, Who will show us any good? Lift up upon us the light of Your countenance, O Jehovah.
  • 有许多人说，谁能指示我们什么好处？耶和华阿，求你仰起脸来，光照我们。
  • 4:7　You have put joy in my heart, More than when the grain and new wine of others abound.
  • 你使我心里快乐，胜过人丰收五谷新酒时的快乐。
  • 4:8　In peace I will both lie down and sleep, For You alone, O Jehovah, cause me to dwell in safety.
  • 我必安然躺下睡觉，因为独有你耶和华使我安然居住。
