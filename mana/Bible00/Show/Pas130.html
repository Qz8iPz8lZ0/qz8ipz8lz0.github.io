诗篇-Psalms 130

Settings

  • 130:0
  • 上行之歌。
  • 130:1　Out of the depths I have called upon You, O Jehovah.
  • 耶和华阿，我从深处向你呼求。
  • 130:2　O Lord, hear my voice; Let Your ears be attentive To the voice of my supplications.
  • 主阿，求你听我的声音；愿你侧耳听我恳求的声音。
  • 130:3　If You, O Jehovah, marked iniquities, Who, O Lord, would stand?
  • 耶和华阿，你若察看罪孽，主阿，谁能站得住呢？
  • 130:4　But with You there is forgiveness, That You would be feared.
  • 但在你有赦免之恩，要叫人敬畏你。
  • 130:5　I wait for Jehovah; my soul waits, And in His word I hope.
  • 我等候耶和华；我的魂等候，我也仰望祂的话。
  • 130:6　My soul waits for the Lord More than watchmen for the morning, Yes, watchmen for the morning.
  • 我的魂等候主，胜于守夜的等候天亮，胜于守夜的等候天亮。
  • 130:7　Hope in Jehovah, O Israel; For with Jehovah there is lovingkindness, And with Him abundantly there is redemption;
  • 以色列阿，你当仰望耶和华；因耶和华有慈爱，在祂那里有丰盛的救赎；
  • 130:8　And it is He who will redeem Israel From all its iniquities.
  • 祂必救赎以色列脱离一切的罪孽。
