诗篇-Psalms 34

Settings

  • 34:0
  • 大卫在亚比米勒面前装疯，被赶逐离去后，所作的诗。
  • 34:1　I will bless Jehovah at all times; His praise will continually be in my mouth.
  • 我要时时颂赞耶和华；赞美祂的话必常在我口中。
  • 34:2　My soul makes its boast in Jehovah; The lowly hear and they rejoice.
  • 我的魂必因耶和华夸耀；卑微的人听见，就要喜乐。
  • 34:3　Magnify Jehovah with me, And let us exalt His name together.
  • 你们要和我一同尊耶和华为大，一同高举祂的名。
  • 34:4　I sought Jehovah, and He answered me; And He delivered me from all that terrified me.
  • 我曾寻求耶和华，祂就应允我，救我脱离一切使我恐惧的。
  • 34:5　They looked to Him and were radiant; And their faces will never be abashed.
  • 人仰望祂，便有光荣；他们的脸必不蒙羞。
  • 34:6　This poor man called out, and Jehovah heard; And He saved him out of all his troubles.
  • 我这穷苦人呼求，耶和华便垂听，救我脱离一切患难。
  • 34:7　The angel of Jehovah encamps Around those who fear Him, and He delivers them.
  • 耶和华的使者，在敬畏祂的人四围安营，搭救他们。
  • 34:8　Taste and see that Jehovah is good; Blessed is the man who takes refuge in Him.
  • 你们要尝尝，便知道耶和华是美善的；投奔于祂的人有福了。
  • 34:9　Fear Jehovah, you who are His saints; For there is no lack with those who fear Him.
  • 耶和华的圣民哪，你们当敬畏祂，因敬畏祂的一无所缺。
  • 34:10　The young lions hunger and starve, But those who seek Jehovah will not lack any good thing.
  • 少壮狮子，还缺食忍饿；但寻求耶和华的，什么好处都不缺。
  • 34:11　Come, children; hear me. I will teach you the fear of Jehovah.
  • 孩子们，你们当来听我的话；我要教导你们敬畏耶和华。
  • 34:12　Who is the man who desires life, Who loves having days in order to see good?
  • 谁是那喜爱生命，爱慕长寿，要享美福的人？
  • 34:13　Guard your tongue from evil, And your lips from speaking deceit.
  • 你要禁止舌头不出恶言，嘴唇不说诡诈的话。
  • 34:14　Turn away from evil and do good; Seek peace and pursue it.
  • 要离恶行善，寻求和睦，一心追赶。
  • 34:15　The eyes of Jehovah are set toward the righteous, And His ears, toward their cry.
  • 耶和华的眼目看顾义人，祂的耳朵听他们的呼求。
  • 34:16　The face of Jehovah is against those who do evil, To cut off the memory of them from the earth.
  • 耶和华向行恶的人变脸，要从地上剪除他们的名号。
  • 34:17　The righteous cry out, and Jehovah hears; And He saves them out of all their troubles.
  • 义人呼求，耶和华听见了，便救他们脱离一切患难。
  • 34:18　Jehovah is near to those who are broken in heart, And He saves those who are contrite in spirit.
  • 耶和华靠近伤心的人，拯救灵里痛悔的人。
  • 34:19　Many are the troubles of the righteous man, But Jehovah delivers him from all of them.
  • 义人多有患难，但耶和华救他脱离这一切；
  • 34:20　He keeps all his bones; Not one of them is broken.
  • 又保全他一身的骨头，连一根也不折断。
  • 34:21　Evil will slay the wicked, And those who hate the righteous man will be condemned.
  • 恶必害死恶人；恨恶义人的，必被定罪。
  • 34:22　Jehovah redeems the soul of His servants, And none who take refuge in Him will be condemned.
  • 耶和华救赎祂仆人的魂；凡投奔于祂的，必不至定罪。
