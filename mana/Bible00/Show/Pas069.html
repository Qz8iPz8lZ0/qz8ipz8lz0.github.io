诗篇-Psalms 69

Settings

  • 69:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长；调用百合花。
  • 69:1　Save me, O God; for the waters Threaten my life.
  • 神阿，求你救我；因为众水威胁我的性命。
  • 69:2　I sink in deep mire, And there is no place to stand; I have come to the depths of water, And the flood flows over me.
  • 我陷在深淤泥中，没有立脚之地；我到了深水中，洪水漫过我身。
  • 69:3　I am weary of my crying; My throat is parched; My eyes fail While I wait for my God.
  • 我因呼求困乏，喉咙发干；我因等候我的神，眼睛失明。
  • 69:4　More numerous than the hairs of my head Are those who hate me without cause; Mighty are those who would destroy me, Those who are wrongly my enemies. What I did not steal, I must then restore.
  • 无故恨我的，比我头发还多；无理与我为仇，要把我除灭的，极为强盛；我没有抢夺的，竟要我偿还。
  • 69:5　O God, You know my foolishness, And my sins of guilt are not hidden from You.
  • 神阿，我的愚昧你原知道；我的罪愆不能向你隐瞒。
  • 69:6　Do not let those who wait on You be put to shame because of me, O Lord Jehovah of hosts; Do not let those who seek You be humiliated because of me, O God of Israel.
  • 万军的主耶和华阿，求你叫那等候你的，不要因我蒙羞；以色列的神阿，求你叫那寻求你的，不要因我受辱。
  • 69:7　Because for Your sake I have borne reproach; Humiliation has covered my face.
  • 因我为你的缘故担受了辱骂，满面羞愧。
  • 69:8　I have become a stranger to my brothers, And a foreigner to my mother's children.
  • 我的兄弟看我是外路人；我母亲的儿子看我是外邦人。
  • 69:9　For the zeal of Your house has devoured me, And the reproaches of those who reproach You have fallen on me.
  • 因我为你的家，心里焦急，如同火烧；并且辱骂你之人的辱骂，都落在我身上。
  • 69:10　When I wept, when my soul was in fasting, That became a reproach to me.
  • 我哭泣，以禁食刻苦我魂，这倒成了我的羞辱。
  • 69:11　When I made sackcloth my clothing, I became a proverb to them.
  • 我拿麻布当衣裳，就成了他们的笑谈。
  • 69:12　Those who sit in the gate talk about me, And I am the mocking song of those who drink liquor.
  • 坐在城门口的谈论我，酒徒也以我为歌曲。
  • 69:13　But as for me, my prayer is to You, O Jehovah, in an acceptable time; O God, in the abundance of Your lovingkindness answer me, In the faithfulness of Your salvation.
  • 但耶和华阿，我在悦纳的时候，向你祷告；神阿，求你按你丰盛的慈爱，凭你拯救的信实，应允我。
  • 69:14　Deliver me out of the mire, and let me not sink; May I be delivered from those who hate me, And out of the depths of water.
  • 求你救我出离淤泥，不让我沉陷下去；求你救我脱离那些恨我的人，救我出离深水。
  • 69:15　Do not let the flood of water flow over me, Nor let the deep swallow me, Nor let the pit close its mouth over me.
  • 求你不让洪水漫过我，不让深渊吞灭我，不让坑坎在我以上合口。
  • 69:16　Answer me, O Jehovah; for Your lovingkindness is good; According to the abundance of Your compassions turn to me.
  • 耶和华阿，求你应允我，因为你的慈爱本为美好；求你按你丰盛的怜恤，回转眷顾我。
  • 69:17　And do not hide Your face from Your servant, For I am in distress; hurry and answer me.
  • 不要向你的仆人掩面，因我是在急难之中；求你速速的应允我。
  • 69:18　Draw near to my soul; redeem it; Ransom me because of my enemies.
  • 求你亲近我，救赎我；求你因我的仇敌把我赎回。
  • 69:19　You know my reproach And my shame and my humiliation; All my adversaries are before You.
  • 你知道我受的辱骂、欺凌、羞辱；我的敌人都在你面前。
  • 69:20　Reproach has broken my heart, And I am sick; I looked for pity, but there was none; And for comforters, but I found none.
  • 辱骂伤破了我的心，我又忧愁难过；我指望有人体恤，却没有一个；我指望有人安慰，却找不着一个。
  • 69:21　But they gave me gall for my food, And for my thirst they gave me vinegar to drink.
  • 他们拿苦胆给我当食物；我渴了，他们拿醋给我喝。
  • 69:22　May their table become a snare before them And a trap when they are at peace.
  • 愿他们的筵席，在他们面前变为网罗，在他们平安的时候，变为陷阱。
  • 69:23　May their eyes be darkened so that they cannot see; And make their loins shake continually.
  • 愿他们的眼睛昏蒙，不能看见；愿你使他们的腰常常战抖。
  • 69:24　Pour out Your indignation upon them; And may the burning fierceness of Your anger overtake them.
  • 求你将你的恼恨，倒在他们身上；愿你的烈怒，追上他们。
  • 69:25　May their camp be desolate; May there be no one to dwell in their tents.
  • 愿他们的营寨，变为荒凉；愿他们的帐棚，无人居住。
  • 69:26　For they persecute him whom You have stricken, And they recount the pain of those whom You have wounded.
  • 因为他们逼迫你所击打的人，数说你所击伤之人的伤痛。
  • 69:27　Add iniquity to their iniquity, And do not let them come into Your righteousness.
  • 求你在他们的罪孽上加罪孽，不要让他们进入你的义。
  • 69:28　May they be blotted out of the book of life, And may they not be written with the righteous.
  • 愿他们从生命册上被涂抹，不得与义人一同登录。
  • 69:29　But I am poor and pained; May Your salvation, O God, set me on high.
  • 但我是困苦伤痛的；神阿，愿你的救恩将我安置在高处。
  • 69:30　I will praise the name of God with a song And will magnify Him with thanksgiving;
  • 我要以诗歌赞美神的名，以感谢尊祂为大；
  • 69:31　And it will be better to Jehovah than an ox Or a bull with horns and hooves.
  • 这便叫耶和华看为美，胜似献牛，或是献有角有蹄的公牛。
  • 69:32　The meek have seen it; they rejoice. You who seek God, let your heart revive.
  • 温柔的人看见了，就喜乐。寻求神的人，愿你们的心苏醒。
  • 69:33　For Jehovah listens to the needy, And He does not despise His prisoners.
  • 因为耶和华垂听了穷乏人，不藐视属祂被囚的人。
  • 69:34　Let the heavens and the earth praise Him, The seas and everything that moves in them.
  • 愿天和地，洋海和其中一切的动物，都赞美祂。
  • 69:35　For God will save Zion And build the cities of Judah; And they will abide there and possess it.
  • 因为神要拯救锡安，建造犹大的城邑；祂的民要在那里居住，得以为业。
  • 69:36　And the seed of His servants will inherit it, And those who love His name will dwell in it.
  • 祂仆人的后裔要承受为业，爱祂名的人也要住在其中。
