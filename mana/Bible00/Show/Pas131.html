诗篇-Psalms 131

Settings

  • 132:0
  • 上行之歌。
  • 131:1　O Jehovah, my heart is not proud, Nor are my eyes haughty; Nor do I go about in things too great Or too wondrous for me.
  • 耶和华阿，我的心不狂傲，我的眼不高大；过于重大和希奇的事，我也不去行。
  • 131:2　Surely I have calmed And quieted my soul, Like a weaned child with its mother; Like a weaned child is my soul within me.
  • 我使我的魂平稳安静，好像断过奶的孩子在他母亲怀中；我的魂在我里面真像断过奶的孩子。
  • 131:3　Hope in Jehovah, O Israel, From now and to eternity.
  • 以色列阿，你当仰望耶和华，从今时直到永远。
