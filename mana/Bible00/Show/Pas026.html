诗篇-Psalms 26

Settings

  • 26:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 26:1　Judge me, O Jehovah; For I have walked in my integrity, And in Jehovah I have trusted; I will not waver.
  • 耶和华阿，求你为我伸冤，因我向来行事纯全，并且信靠耶和华；我必不摇动。
  • 26:2　Examine me, O Jehovah, and try me; Test my inward parts and my heart.
  • 耶和华阿，求你察看我，试验我，熬炼我的肺腑心肠。
  • 26:3　For Your lovingkindness is before my eyes, And I have walked in Your truth.
  • 因为你的慈爱常在我眼前，我也按你的真理而行。
  • 26:4　I do not sit with men of falsehood, Nor will I associate with hypocrites.
  • 我没有和虚谎人同坐，也不与假冒为善的人往来。
  • 26:5　I hate the assembly of evildoers, Nor will I sit with the wicked.
  • 我恨恶作恶之人的会，必不与恶人同坐。
  • 26:6　I wash my hands in innocence And go around Your altar, O Jehovah,
  • 耶和华阿，我要洗手表明无辜，好环绕你的祭坛，
  • 26:7　Making myself heard with the voice of thanksgiving And telling out all Your wonderful deeds.
  • 发出称谢的声音，并述说你一切奇妙的作为。
  • 26:8　O Jehovah, I love the habitation of Your house, And the place where Your glory abides.
  • 耶和华阿，我爱你所住的殿，和你的荣耀所居之处。
  • 26:9　Do not gather my soul with sinners, Nor my life with men of bloodshed,
  • 不要把我的魂和罪人一同除掉，也不要把我的性命和流人血的一同除掉。
  • 26:10　In whose hand is a wicked scheme, And whose right hand is full of bribes.
  • 他们的手中有恶计，右手满有贿赂。
  • 26:11　But as for me, I walk in my integrity; Redeem me and be gracious to me.
  • 至于我，我行事纯全；求你救赎我，恩待我。
  • 26:12　My foot stands on level ground; In the assemblies I will bless Jehovah.
  • 我的脚站在平坦地方；在众会中我要颂赞耶和华。
