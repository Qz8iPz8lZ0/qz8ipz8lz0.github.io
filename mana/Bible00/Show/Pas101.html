诗篇-Psalms 101

Settings

  • 101:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 101:1　I will sing of lovingkindness and justice; To You, O Jehovah, I will sing psalms.
  • 我要歌唱慈爱和公平；耶和华阿，我要向你歌颂。
  • 101:2　I will give careful heed to the way of the perfect. When will You come to me? I will walk in the perfectness of my heart Within my house.
  • 我要谨慎遵行完全的道路。你几时到我这里来呢？我要存完全的心，行在我家中。
  • 101:3　I will not set anything base Before my eyes; I hate what those who turn aside do; It will not cling to me.
  • 卑下的事，我都不摆在我眼前；偏离正路之人所作的事，我甚恨恶，不容沾在我身上。
  • 101:4　A perverse heart will depart from me; No evil will I know.
  • 弯曲的心必远离我；一切的恶事，我不认识。
  • 101:5　He who secretly slanders his neighbor, Him I will destroy; He who is of a haughty look and a proud heart, Him I will not tolerate.
  • 在暗中谗谤他邻居的，我必灭绝；眼目高傲，心里骄纵的，我必不容忍。
  • 101:6　My eyes are on the faithful of the land, That they may abide with me; He who walks in the way of the perfect, He will minister to me.
  • 我眼要看这地忠信的人，叫他们与我同住；行在完全道路上的，他要伺候我。
  • 101:7　He who practices deceit Will not dwell within my house; He who speaks lies will not be maintained In my sight.
  • 行诡诈的，必不得住在我家里；说谎话的，必不得在我眼前坚立。
  • 101:8　Morning by morning I will destroy All the wicked of the land, In order to cut off from the city of Jehovah All the workers of iniquity.
  • 我每日早晨，要灭绝这地所有的恶人，好把一切作孽的，从耶和华的城里剪除。
