诗篇-Psalms 13

Settings

  • 13:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 13:1　How long, O Jehovah? Will You forget me continually? How long will You hide Your face from me?
  • 耶和华阿，你忘记我要到几时呢？要到永远么？你掩面不顾我要到几时呢？
  • 13:2　How long will I bear concern in my soul And sorrow in my heart day after day? How long will my enemy be exalted over me?
  • 我魂里筹算，心里终日愁苦，要到几时呢？我的仇敌高居我上，要到几时呢？
  • 13:3　Consider and answer me, O Jehovah my God; Enlighten my eyes lest I sleep the sleep of death,
  • 耶和华我的神阿，求你看顾我，应允我；求你使我眼目光明，免得我沉睡至死；
  • 13:4　Lest my enemy say, I have prevailed against him, And my adversaries exult when I am shaken.
  • 免得我的仇敌说，我胜过了他；免得我的敌人在我摇动的时候欢乐。
  • 13:5　But I trust in Your lovingkindness; My heart will exult in Your salvation;
  • 但我信靠你的慈爱；我的心要因你的救恩欢乐；
  • 13:6　I will sing to Jehovah, For He has dealt bountifully with me.
  • 我要向耶和华歌唱，因祂用厚恩待我。
