诗篇-Psalms 145

Settings

  • 145:0
  • 大卫的赞美歌。
  • 145:1　I will extol You, O my God and King; And I will bless Your name forever and ever.
  • 我的神我的王阿，我要尊崇你；我要永永远远颂赞你的名。
  • 145:2　Every day I will bless You, And I will praise Your name forever and ever.
  • 我要天天颂赞你，我要永永远远赞美你的名。
  • 145:3　Great is Jehovah, and much to be praised; And His greatness is unsearchable.
  • 耶和华本为大，该受大赞美；其大无法测度。
  • 145:4　One generation will laud Your works to another, And they will declare Your mighty acts.
  • 这一代要对另一代称颂你的作为，他们要传扬你的大能。
  • 145:5　Upon the glorious splendor of Your majesty And upon Your wondrous works I will muse.
  • 我要默想你威严的尊荣，和你奇妙的作为。
  • 145:6　And men will speak of the might of Your awesome deeds, And I will declare Your greatness.
  • 人要讲说你可畏之事的能力，我也要述说你的伟大。
  • 145:7　They will utter the memory of Your great goodness And will sing for joy of Your righteousness.
  • 他们要记念且说出你至大的美善，并要欢唱你的公义。
  • 145:8　Jehovah is gracious and compassionate, Long-suffering and great in lovingkindness.
  • 耶和华有恩典，有怜恤，不轻易发怒，大有慈爱。
  • 145:9　Jehovah is good to all, And His compassions are upon all His works.
  • 耶和华善待万有，祂的怜恤覆庇祂一切所造的。
  • 145:10　All Your works will praise You, O Jehovah; And Your faithful ones will bless You.
  • 耶和华阿，你一切所造的，都要赞美你；你的虔诚人也要颂赞你。
  • 145:11　They will speak of the glory of Your kingdom And tell of Your power,
  • 他们要讲说你国的荣耀，谈论你的大能，
  • 145:12　To make known to the sons of men Your mighty acts And the glorious splendor of Your kingdom.
  • 好叫世人知道你大能的作为，并你国度的威荣。
  • 145:13　Your kingdom is an eternal kingdom, And Your dominion is throughout all generations.
  • 你的国是永远的国，你的管治权存到万代。
  • 145:14　Jehovah upholds all who are falling And raises up all who are bowed down.
  • 耶和华扶持一切跌倒的人，扶起一切被压下的人。
  • 145:15　The eyes of everyone wait for You, And You give them their food in due season.
  • 众人的眼目都仰望你，你按时给他们食物。
  • 145:16　You open up Your hand And satisfy the desire of every living thing.
  • 你张开手，使一切有生命的物都随愿饱足。
  • 145:17　Jehovah is righteous in all His ways, And faithfully kind in all His doings.
  • 耶和华在祂一切所行的，无不公义；在祂一切所作的，都有慈爱。
  • 145:18　Jehovah is near to all who call upon Him, To all who call upon Him in truth.
  • 凡呼求耶和华的，就是真实呼求祂的，耶和华便与他们相近。
  • 145:19　He will fulfill the desire of those who fear Him, And He will hear their cry and save them.
  • 敬畏祂的，祂必成就他们的心愿，也必听他们的呼求，拯救他们。
  • 145:20　Jehovah preserves all who love Him, But He will destroy all the wicked.
  • 耶和华保护一切爱祂的人，却要灭绝一切的恶人。
  • 145:21　My mouth will speak the praise of Jehovah, And all flesh will bless His holy name forever and ever.
  • 我的口要说出赞美耶和华的话，凡有血肉的都要永永远远颂赞祂的圣名。
