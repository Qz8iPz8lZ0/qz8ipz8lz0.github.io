诗篇-Psalms 105

Settings

  • 105:1　Give thanks to Jehovah; call upon His name; Make known His deeds among the peoples.
  • 你们要称谢耶和华，呼求祂的名，在万民中传扬祂的作为。
  • 105:2　Sing to Him; sing psalms to Him; Muse upon all His wondrous deeds.
  • 要向祂唱诗、歌颂，谈论祂一切奇妙的作为。
  • 105:3　Glory in His holy name; Let the heart of those who seek Jehovah rejoice.
  • 要因祂的圣名夸耀；寻求耶和华的人，心中应当喜乐。
  • 105:4　Seek Jehovah and His strength; Seek His face continually.
  • 要寻求耶和华与祂的能力，时常寻求祂的面。
  • 105:5　Remember His wondrous deeds, which He has done, His signs and the judgments of His mouth,
  • 祂仆人亚伯拉罕的后裔，祂所拣选雅各的子孙哪，你们要记念祂所行奇妙的作为，和祂的奇事，并祂口中的判语。

    105:6

    O seed of Abraham His servant, O children of Jacob, His chosen ones.
  • 105:7　He is Jehovah our God; His judgments are in all the earth.
  • 祂是耶和华我们的神，全地都有祂的判断。
  • 105:8　He has remembered His covenant forever, The word that He commanded to a thousand generations,
  • 祂记念祂的约，直到永远；记念祂所吩咐的话，直到千代，
  • 105:9　The covenant that He made with Abraham, And His oath unto Isaac.
  • 就是祂与亚伯拉罕所立的约，向以撒所起的誓。
  • 105:10　And He confirmed it unto Jacob as a statute, Unto Israel as an eternal covenant,
  • 祂又将这约向雅各定为律例，向以色列定为永远的约，
  • 105:11　Saying, To you I will give the land of Canaan, The portion of your inheritance;
  • 说，我必将迦南地赐给你，作你们产业的分。
  • 105:12　When they were men few in number, Indeed very few and strangers in the land.
  • 当时他们人数有限，非常稀少，并且在那地为寄居的。
  • 105:13　And they went about from nation to nation, From one kingdom to another people.
  • 他们从这邦游到那邦，从这国行到那国。
  • 105:14　He allowed no man to oppress them; Indeed He rebuked kings for their sake.
  • 祂不容什么人欺压他们，为他们的缘故责备君王，
  • 105:15　Saying, Do not touch My anointed ones, And do My prophets no harm.
  • 说，不可触犯我所膏的人，也不可加害我的申言者。
  • 105:16　And He called a famine upon the land; He broke the whole staff of bread.
  • 祂命饥荒降在那地上，将所倚靠的粮食，全行断绝。
  • 105:17　He sent a man before them; Joseph was sold as a servant.
  • 祂在他们以先打发一个人去；约瑟被卖为奴仆。
  • 105:18　They afflicted his feet with fetters; He himself was placed in irons.
  • 人用脚镣伤他的脚，他被铁链捆拘。
  • 105:19　Until the time when his word came to pass, The word of Jehovah tried him.
  • 耶和华的话试炼他，直等到他所说的应验了。
  • 105:20　The king sent men and freed him, Even the ruler of the peoples, and let him go.
  • 王打发人把他解开，治理众民的，把他释放了；
  • 105:21　He made him lord of his house And ruler over all his possessions,
  • 立他作王家之主，掌管王一切所有的，
  • 105:22　To imprison his princes at will, And to teach his elders wisdom.
  • 使他随意捆绑王的臣宰，将智慧教导王的长老。
  • 105:23　Then Israel came into Egypt, And Jacob sojourned in the land of Ham.
  • 于是以色列到了埃及，雅各在含地寄居。
  • 105:24　And He made His people very fruitful, And caused them to be mightier than their adversaries.
  • 耶和华使祂的百姓极其繁多，使他们比敌人强盛；
  • 105:25　He turned their heart to hate His people, To deal craftily with His servants.
  • 使敌人的心转去恨祂的百姓，并用诡计待祂的仆人。
  • 105:26　He sent Moses His servant, And Aaron, whom He had chosen.
  • 祂打发祂的仆人摩西，和祂所拣选的亚伦。
  • 105:27　They set His signs among them, And wonders in the land of Ham.
  • 他们在敌人中间施行祂的神迹，在含地施行祂的奇事。
  • 105:28　He sent darkness and made it dark; And they did not rebel against His words.
  • 祂打发黑暗，使那地黑暗；他们没有违背祂的话。
  • 105:29　He turned their waters into blood, And killed their fish.
  • 祂叫埃及的水变为血，叫他们的鱼死了。
  • 105:30　Their land teemed with frogs, Even in the chambers of their kings.
  • 在他们的地上，甚至在他们君王的内室，青蛙多多滋生。
  • 105:31　He spoke, and a swarm of flies came; There were gnats within all their borders.
  • 祂说一声，苍蝇就成群而来，并且他们四境之内都有了虱子。
  • 105:32　He gave them hail for rain, And flaming fire in their land.
  • 祂给他们降下冰雹为雨，在他们的地上降下火焰。
  • 105:33　He struck their vines and their fig trees, And He broke the trees of their borders.
  • 祂也击打他们的葡萄树和无花果树，打坏他们境内的树木。
  • 105:34　He spoke, and the swarming locusts came, And the licking locusts, even without number.
  • 祂说一声，群蝗和舔蝗就来，不计其数，
  • 105:35　And they ate every herb in their land, And they ate the fruit of their ground.
  • 吃尽了他们地上各样的菜蔬，和土地的出产。
  • 105:36　He struck all the firstborn in their land, The first of all their vigor.
  • 祂又击杀他们那地一切的长子，就是他们强壮时头生的。
  • 105:37　And He brought them out with silver and gold, And there was none among His tribes who stumbled.
  • 祂领自己的百姓带银子金子出来，祂支派中没有一个软弱的。
  • 105:38　Egypt rejoiced at their going forth, For a dread of them had fallen upon them.
  • 他们出来的时候，埃及人便欢喜；因为埃及人惧怕他们。
  • 105:39　He spread a cloud for a covering, And a fire to give light by night.
  • 祂铺张云彩当遮盖，夜间使火光照。
  • 105:40　They asked, and He brought quails; And He satisfied them with the bread of heaven.
  • 他们一求，祂就使鹌鹑飞来，并用天上的粮食，叫他们饱足。
  • 105:41　He opened the rock, and water gushed forth; It ran in the dry places like a river.
  • 祂打开磐石，水就涌出；在干旱之处，水流成河。
  • 105:42　For He remembered His holy word And Abraham His servant.
  • 这都因祂记念祂的圣言，和祂的仆人亚伯拉罕。
  • 105:43　And He brought forth His people with gladness, His chosen ones with a ringing shout.
  • 祂带领百姓欢乐而出，带领选民欢呼前往。
  • 105:44　And He gave them the lands of the nations, And they took possession of the labor of the peoples,
  • 祂将列国的地赐给他们，他们便承受众民劳碌得来的，
  • 105:45　So that they might keep His statutes And observe His laws. Hallelujah.
  • 好使他们谨守祂的律例，遵守祂的律法。阿利路亚。
