诗篇-Psalms 86

Settings

  • 86:0
  • 大卫的祷告。
  • 86:1　Incline Your ear, O Jehovah; answer me; For I am poor and needy.
  • 耶和华阿，求你侧耳应允我，因我是困苦穷乏的。
  • 86:2　Keep my soul; for I am faithful; Save Your servant who trusts in You, O You who are my God.
  • 求你保存我的性命；因我是忠信的。你是我的神，求你拯救这信靠你的仆人。
  • 86:3　Be gracious to me, O Lord; For to You do I call out all day long.
  • 主阿，求你恩待我，因我终日呼求你。
  • 86:4　Cause the soul of Your servant to rejoice, For unto You, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.
  • 主阿，求你使仆人的魂喜乐；因为我的魂仰望你。
  • 86:5　For You, O Lord, are good and ready to forgive And abundant in lovingkindness to all who call upon You.
  • 主阿，你本为良善，乐意饶恕人，对一切呼求你的人，有丰盛的慈爱。
  • 86:6　Give ear, O Jehovah, to my prayer, And give heed to the voice of my supplications.
  • 耶和华阿，求你侧耳听我的祷告，垂听我恳求的声音。
  • 86:7　In the day of my distress I call upon You, For You answer me.
  • 我在患难之日要呼求你，因为你必应允我。
  • 86:8　There is none like You among the gods, O Lord; Nor are there any works like Yours.
  • 主阿，诸神之中没有可比你的；你的作为也无可比。
  • 86:9　All the nations which You have made will come And worship before You, O Lord; And they will glorify Your name.
  • 主阿，你所造的万邦，都要来到你面前下拜；他们也要荣耀你的名。
  • 86:10　For You are great, and You do wondrous deeds; You alone are God.
  • 因你为大，且行奇妙的事；惟独你是神。
  • 86:11　Teach me, O Jehovah, Your way; I will walk in Your truth. Make my heart single in fearing Your name.
  • 耶和华阿，求你将你的道路指教我；我要行在你的真理中。求你使我专心敬畏你的名。
  • 86:12　I will praise You, O Lord my God, with all my heart; And I will glorify Your name forever.
  • 主我的神阿，我要全心赞美你；我要荣耀你的名，直到永远。
  • 86:13　For Your lovingkindness is great toward me, And You have delivered my soul from the lowest Sheol.
  • 因为你向我发的慈爱是大的，你救了我的魂，免入极深的阴间。
  • 86:14　O God, the proud have risen up against me, And a company of violent men seek my life; And they do not set You before them.
  • 神阿，骄傲的人起来攻击我，又有一伙强横的人寻索我的命；他们不将你摆在面前。
  • 86:15　But You, O Lord, are a compassionate and gracious God, Long-suffering and abundant in lovingkindness and truth.
  • 但主阿，你是有怜恤有恩典的神，不轻易发怒，并有丰盛的慈爱和真实。
  • 86:16　Turn to me and be gracious to me; Give Your strength to Your servant, And save the son of Your female servant.
  • 求你转向我，恩待我；将你的力量赐给你仆人，救你婢女的儿子。
  • 86:17　Show me a token of goodness, That those who hate me may see it and be ashamed; For You, O Jehovah, have helped me and comforted me.
  • 求你向我显出善待我的凭据，叫恨我的人看见便羞愧；因为你耶和华帮助了我，安慰了我。
