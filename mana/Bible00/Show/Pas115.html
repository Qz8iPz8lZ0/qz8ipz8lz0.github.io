诗篇-Psalms 115

Settings

  • 115:1　Not to us, O Jehovah, not to us, But to Your name give glory, For the sake of Your lovingkindness and Your truth.
  • 耶和华阿，荣耀不要归与我们，不要归与我们，要因你的慈爱和真实，归与你的名。
  • 115:2　Why should the nations say, Where then is their God?
  • 为何容外邦人说，他们的神在那里呢？
  • 115:3　But our God is in the heavens; He does whatever He pleases.
  • 然而我们的神在天上；祂随自己的喜悦行事。
  • 115:4　Their idols are mere silver and gold, The work of human hands.
  • 他们的偶像不过是金的银的，是人手所造的；
  • 115:5　They have mouths, but they do not speak; They have eyes, but they do not see.
  • 有口却不能言，有眼却不能看，
  • 115:6　They have ears, but they do not hear; They have noses, but they do not smell.
  • 有耳却不能听，有鼻却不能闻，
  • 115:7　They have hands, but they do not feel; They have feet, but they do not walk; They make no sound in their throat.
  • 有手却不能摸，有脚却不能走，喉咙也不能出声。
  • 115:8　Those who make them, All who trust in them, Are like them.
  • 造它们的，并一切倚靠它们的，都要和它们一样。
  • 115:9　O Israel, trust in Jehovah. He is their help and their shield.
  • 以色列阿，当信靠耶和华；祂是他们的帮助，和他们的盾牌。
  • 115:10　O house of Aaron, trust in Jehovah. He is their help and their shield.
  • 亚伦家阿，当信靠耶和华；祂是他们的帮助，和他们的盾牌。
  • 115:11　O you who fear Jehovah, trust in Jehovah. He is their help and their shield.
  • 你们敬畏耶和华的，当信靠耶和华；祂是他们的帮助，和他们的盾牌。
  • 115:12　Jehovah has been mindful of us; He will bless us; He will bless the house of Israel; He will bless the house of Aaron;
  • 耶和华向来记念我们；祂还要赐福给我们，要赐福给以色列家，赐福给亚伦家；
  • 115:13　He will bless those who fear Jehovah, Both small and great.
  • 凡敬畏耶和华的，无论大小，祂必赐福给他们。
  • 115:14　May Jehovah give you increase, Both you and your children.
  • 愿耶和华叫你们和你们的子孙，日见加增。
  • 115:15　May you be blessed of Jehovah, Maker of heaven and earth.
  • 愿你们蒙造天地之耶和华的福。
  • 115:16　The heavens are the heavens of Jehovah, But He has given the earth to the children of men.
  • 天是耶和华的天；地，祂却给了世人。
  • 115:17　The dead do not praise Jehovah, Nor do any that go down into silence.
  • 死人不能赞美耶和华；下到寂静中的，也都不能。
  • 115:18　But we will bless Jehovah From now and to eternity. Hallelujah.
  • 但我们要颂赞耶和华，从今时直到永远。阿利路亚。
