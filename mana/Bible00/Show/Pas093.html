诗篇-Psalms 93

Settings

  • 93:1　Jehovah reigns; He is clothed with majesty. Jehovah is clothed, He has girded Himself, with strength. Indeed the world has been established; it will not be moved.
  • 耶和华作王；祂以威严为衣穿上。耶和华以能力为衣，以能力束腰。世界就坚立，必不动摇。
  • 93:2　Your throne is established from of old; You are from eternity.
  • 你的宝座自古就立定；你从亘古就有。
  • 93:3　The floods have lifted up, O Jehovah; The floods have lifted up their voice; The floods lift up their roaring.
  • 耶和华阿，大水扬起了；大水扬起了声音，扬起了波浪的响声。
  • 93:4　More than the voices of many waters, Than the mighty breakers of the sea, Jehovah on high is mighty!
  • 耶和华在高处大有能力，胜过众水的声音，胜过洋海的大浪！
  • 93:5　Your testimonies are very sure; Holiness befits Your house, O Jehovah, forevermore.
  • 耶和华阿，你的法度极为确定；你的殿称为圣别，是合宜的，直到永远。
