诗篇-Psalms 66

Settings

  • 66:0
  • 一篇诗，一首歌，交与歌咏长。
  • 66:1　Shout joyfully to God, all the earth;
  • 全地都当向神欢呼，
  • 66:2　Sing forth the glory of His name; Make glorious His praise.
  • 歌颂祂名的荣耀，使献给祂的赞美显为荣耀。
  • 66:3　Say to God, How awesome are Your deeds! Because of the greatness of Your strength, Your enemies come cringing to You.
  • 当对神说，你的作为何等可畏！因你的大能，仇敌要归顺你。
  • 66:4　All the earth will worship You And sing psalms to You; They will sing psalms to Your name.
  • 全地都要敬拜你，歌颂你；他们要歌颂你的名。　细拉
  • 66:5　Come and see the works of God; He is awesome in His deeds toward the sons of men.
  • 你们来看神所作的；祂向世人所行之事，是可畏的。
  • 66:6　He turned the sea into dry land; Through the river they crossed on foot. There we rejoiced in Him.
  • 祂将海变成干地；众人步行过河。我们在那里因祂喜乐。
  • 66:7　He rules by His might forever; His eyes observe the nations: Let not the rebellious exalt themselves.
  • 祂用权能治理众民，直到永远；祂的眼睛鉴察列邦：悖逆的人不可自高。　细拉
  • 66:8　Bless our God, O peoples; And make the voice of His praise heard,
  • 众民哪，你们当颂赞我们的神，使人得听赞美祂的声音；
  • 66:9　Who has kept our soul in life And does not allow our feet to slip.
  • 祂使我们的性命存活，也不叫我们的脚滑跌。
  • 66:10　For You have tried us, O God; You have refined us as silver is refined.
  • 神阿，你曾试验我们，熬炼我们，如熬炼银子一样。
  • 66:11　You have brought us into the net; You have put a heavy burden on our loins.
  • 你使我们进入网罗，把重担放在我们身上。
  • 66:12　You have made men ride over our heads; We have gone through fire and water; But You have brought us forth into abundance.
  • 你使人坐车轧我们的头；我们经过水火；你却领我们出到丰富之地。
  • 66:13　I will come into Your house with burnt offerings; I will pay my vows to You,
  • 我要凭燔祭进入你的殿；我要向你还我的愿，
  • 66:14　Which my lips uttered And my mouth spoke when I was in distress.
  • 就是在急难时我嘴唇所发的，口中所许的。
  • 66:15　I will offer to You burnt offerings of fatlings With the smoke of rams; I will offer cattle with goats.
  • 我要把肥畜作燔祭，连同公羊的香气献给你；我要把公牛连同山羊献上。　细拉
  • 66:16　Come, hear, all you who fear God, And I will recount what He has done for my soul.
  • 凡敬畏神的人，你们都来听，我要述说祂为我所行的事。
  • 66:17　To Him I called out with my mouth, And He was extolled with my tongue.
  • 我曾用口呼求祂，我的舌头也颂扬祂。
  • 66:18　If I regarded iniquity in my heart, The Lord did not hear.
  • 我若心里注重罪孽，主必不听。
  • 66:19　But indeed God has heard; He has given heed to the voice of my prayer.
  • 但神实在听见了；祂垂听了我祷告的声音。
  • 66:20　Blessed be God, Who has not turned my prayer away Nor His lovingkindness from me.
  • 神是当受颂赞的，祂并没有推却我的祷告，也没有叫祂的慈爱离开我。
