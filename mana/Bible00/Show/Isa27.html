以赛亚书-Isaiah 27

Settings

  • 27:1　In that day Jehovah will punish, With His harsh, great, and mighty sword, Leviathan the fleeing serpent, And leviathan the crooked serpent; And He will slay the dragon that is in the sea.
  • 到那日，耶和华必用祂刚硬、巨大、有力的刀，刑罚鳄鱼，就是那快行的蛇；刑罚鳄鱼，就是那曲行的蛇；并杀海中的大龙。
  • 27:2　In that day: A vineyard of wine, sing of it!
  • 当那日有出酒的葡萄园；你们要指这园唱歌，说，
  • 27:3　I, Jehovah, am its keeper; Every moment I water it. Lest anyone visit harm upon it, By night and by day I guard it.
  • 我耶和华是看守葡萄园的，我必时刻浇灌，昼夜看守，免得有人侵害。
  • 27:4　I have no wrath. Oh, that I had thorns and thistles! In battle I would march against them; I would burn them completely.
  • 我没有忿怒。谁给我长出荆棘蒺藜，我就勇往直前与他争战，将他全部焚烧。
  • 27:5　Or let him grasp at My strength; Let him make peace with Me; Let him make peace with Me.
  • 不然，让他持住我的能力，使他与我和好；愿他与我和好。
  • 27:6　In the days to come Jacob will take root; Israel will blossom and bud; And they will fill the surface of the world with their produce.
  • 将来雅各要扎根，以色列要发芽开花；他们的果实必遍满世界。
  • 27:7　Has He struck them like those who struck them? Have they been slain like those slain by Him?
  • 主击打他们，岂像那些击打他们的人么？他们被杀戮，岂像那些被祂杀戮的人么？
  • 27:8　By expulsion and by banishing them, You contended with them; He sent them away with His harsh wind in the day of the east wind.
  • 你赶逐他们、遣开他们，是要与他们相争；刮东风的日子，祂就用暴风将他们逐去。
  • 27:9　Thus by this the iniquity of Jacob will be expiated, And this is all the fruit of the removal of their sin; When they make all the stones of their altar Like chalk stones beaten to powder-Asherahs and sun images will not stand.
  • 所以雅各的罪孽得赦免，他的罪得除掉的果效，全在于此：就是他叫祭坛的石头变为打碎的灰石，以致木像和日像不再立起。
  • 27:10　For the fortified city is isolated; The habitation is given up and abandoned like a wilderness. There the calf will feed, And there he will lie down and consume its branches.
  • 因为坚固城被孤立，居住之处被撇下离弃，像旷野一样。牛犊必在那里吃草，在那里躺卧，并吃尽其中的树枝。
  • 27:11　When its boughs are withered, they will be broken off; Women come and make a fire with them; For they are not a people of understanding. Therefore He who made them will not have compassion on them, And He who formed them will not show favor to them.
  • 枝条枯干，必被折断；妇女要来，拿去生火；因为这百姓蒙昧无知，所以造作他们的，必不怜恤他们；塑造他们的，也不施恩与他们。
  • 27:12　And in that day Jehovah will beat out the grain from the flowing stream of the River Unto the brook of Egypt; And you will be gleaned One by one, O you children of Israel.
  • 以色列人哪，到那日，耶和华必从大河的溪流，直到埃及小河，收打谷物，你们就要一个一个被收集。
  • 27:13　And in that day a great trumpet will be blown, And the ones lost in the land of Assyria will come, As well as the outcasts in the land of Egypt, And they will worship Jehovah On the holy mountain in Jerusalem.
  • 当那日，必大发角声；在亚述地将要灭亡的，并在埃及地被赶散的，都要来；他们就在耶路撒冷圣山上敬拜耶和华。
