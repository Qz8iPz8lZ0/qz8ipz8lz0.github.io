诗篇-Psalms 59

Settings

  • 59:0
  • 扫罗打发人窥探大卫的房屋，要杀他；那时大卫作了这金诗，交与歌咏长；调用休要毁坏。
  • 59:1　Deliver me from my enemies, O my God; Set me on high, secure from those who rise up against me.
  • 我的神阿，求你救我脱离仇敌，把我安置在高处，脱离那些起来攻击我的人。
  • 59:2　Deliver me from the workers of iniquity, And save me from men of bloodshed.
  • 求你救我脱离作孽的人，救我脱离流人血的人。
  • 59:3　For behold, they lie in wait for my soul; The strong gather themselves against me. Not because of any transgression of mine nor because of any sin of mine, O Jehovah,
  • 因为看哪，他们埋伏，要害我的命；有能力的人聚集攻击我。耶和华阿，这不是为我的过犯，也不是为我的罪。
  • 59:4　Not for any fault of mine, they run and ready themselves; Awake to meet me, and see.
  • 我虽然无过，他们还是跑来，预备好攻击我；求你醒起，前来帮助我，并且察看。
  • 59:5　Indeed You, O Jehovah, God of hosts, God of Israel, Arise to punish all the nations; Have no mercy upon any who treacherously commit iniquity.
  • 万军之神耶和华，以色列的神阿，求你兴起，惩罚万邦；不要怜悯一切诡诈作孽的人。　细拉
  • 59:6　They return in the evening; They growl like a dog And go around the city.
  • 他们晚上转回，叫号如狗，围城绕行。
  • 59:7　Behold, they belch forth with their mouth; Swords are in their lips; For, Who, they say, hears?
  • 看哪，他们口中喷吐恶言，嘴里有刀；他们说，有谁听见？
  • 59:8　But You, O Jehovah, laugh at them; You have all the nations in derision.
  • 但你耶和华必向他们发笑；你要嗤笑万邦。
  • 59:9　O my strength, I will watch for You; For God is my high retreat.
  • 我的力量阿，我必儆醒等候你；因为神是我的高台。
  • 59:10　My God in His lovingkindness will meet me; God will let me look triumphantly upon those who lie in wait for me.
  • 我的神要以慈爱迎接我；神要叫我看见那些埋伏等候我的人遭报。
  • 59:11　Do not slay them, lest my people forget; Scatter them by Your power, and bring them down, O Lord our shield.
  • 不要杀他们，恐怕我的民忘记；主阿，你是我们的盾牌，求你用你的能力使他们四散，且降为卑。
  • 59:12　For the sin of their mouth, For the word of their lips, Let them even be taken in their arrogance, And because of the cursing and the lying that they speak.
  • 因他们口中的罪，和嘴里的言语，并所说咒骂虚谎的话，愿他们在自己的狂傲中被缠住。
  • 59:13　Consume them in wrath; consume them so that they are no more; And men will know that God rules in Jacob Unto the ends of the earth.
  • 求你在怒中消灭他们；消灭他们以至于无有；人必知道神在雅各中间掌权，直到地极。　细拉
  • 59:14　They return in the evening; They growl like a dog And go around the city.
  • 到了晚上，他们转回；他们叫号如狗，围城绕行。
  • 59:15　They wander about for food; And if they are not filled, they linger.
  • 他们走来走去，寻找食物；若不得饱，就流连在外。
  • 59:16　But I will sing of Your strength; And I will joyfully sing of Your lovingkindness in the morning. For You have been my high retreat And a refuge in the day of my distress.
  • 但我要歌唱你的力量；早晨我要欢唱你的慈爱；因为你作过我的高台，在我急难的日子，作过我的避难所。
  • 59:17　O my strength, unto You I will sing psalms, For God is my high retreat, My God of lovingkindness.
  • 我的力量阿，我要歌颂你，因为神是我的高台，是向我施慈爱的神。
