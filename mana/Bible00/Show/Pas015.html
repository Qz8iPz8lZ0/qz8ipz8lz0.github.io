诗篇-Psalms 15

Settings

  • 15:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 15:1　O Jehovah, who may sojourn in Your tent? Who may dwell on Your holy mountain?
  • 耶和华阿，谁能寄居你的帐幕？谁能住在你的圣山？
  • 15:2　He who walks in integrity And does righteousness And speaks truth from his heart.
  • 就是行为纯全，作事公义，从心里说实话的人。
  • 15:3　He does not slander with his tongue; He does not do evil to his friend, Nor does he take up a reproach against his neighbor.
  • 他不以舌头谗谤人，不恶待朋友，也不随伙辱骂邻里。
  • 15:4　In his eyes a reprobate is despised, But he honors those who fear Jehovah. Should he swear to his harm, He does not change.
  • 他眼中藐视可弃绝的人，却尊重敬畏耶和华的人。他发了誓，虽然自己吃亏，也不更改。
  • 15:5　He does not lend his money on interest, Nor accept a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things will not be shaken forever.
  • 他不放债取利，也不受贿赂以害无辜。行这些事的人，必永不动摇。
