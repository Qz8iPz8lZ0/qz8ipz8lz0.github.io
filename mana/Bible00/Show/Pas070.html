诗篇-Psalms 70

Settings

  • 70:0
  • 大卫的记念诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 70:1　O God, hasten to deliver me; O Jehovah, hasten to help me.
  • 神阿，求你快快搭救我；耶和华阿，求你快快帮助我。
  • 70:2　May those who seek my life Be put to shame and confounded; May those who take delight in my misfortune Be turned back and humiliated;
  • 愿那些寻索我命的，抱愧蒙羞；愿那些喜悦我遭难的，退后受辱。
  • 70:3　May those who say, Aha! Aha! Turn away because of their shame;
  • 愿那些对我说，阿哈，阿哈的，因羞愧退去。
  • 70:4　May all those who seek You Be glad and rejoice in You; And may those who love Your salvation Say continually, May God be magnified!
  • 愿一切寻求你的，因你欢喜快乐；愿那些爱你救恩的，常说，当尊神为大！
  • 70:5　But I am poor and needy; O God, hasten to me. You are my help and my Deliverer; O Jehovah, do not delay.
  • 但我是困苦穷乏的；神阿，求你速速到我这里来。你是帮助我的，搭救我的；耶和华阿，求你不要耽延。
