诗篇-Psalms 62

Settings

  • 62:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长耶杜顿。
  • 62:1　My soul waits in silence for God alone; My salvation is from Him.
  • 我的魂默默无声，专等候神；我的救恩是从祂而来。
  • 62:2　He alone is my rock and my salvation, My high retreat; I will not be greatly shaken.
  • 惟独祂是我的磐石和我的拯救，是我的高台；我必不很动摇。
  • 62:3　How long will you assail a man So that you may slay him, all of you, Like a leaning wall or a tottering fence?
  • 你们大家攻击一人，把他杀害，如同毁坏歪斜的墙，将倒的壁，要到几时呢？
  • 62:4　They only make plans to thrust him down from his dignity; They take delight in lies; With their mouth they bless, But inwardly they curse.
  • 他们彼此商议，专要从他的尊位上把他推下；他们喜爱谎话；口虽祝福，心里却咒诅。　细拉
  • 62:5　My soul, wait in silence for God alone, For my expectation is from Him.
  • 我的魂哪，你当默默无声，专等候神，因为我的盼望是从祂而来。
  • 62:6　He alone is my rock and my salvation, My high retreat; I will not be shaken.
  • 惟独祂是我的磐石和我的拯救，是我的高台，我必不动摇。
  • 62:7　On God my salvation and my glory depend; The rock of my strength, my refuge, is in God.
  • 我的拯救和我的荣耀，都在于神；我力量的磐石，我的避难所，是在神里面。
  • 62:8　Trust in Him at all times, O people; Pour out your heart before Him; God is a refuge to us.
  • 百姓阿，你们当时时信靠祂，在祂面前倾心吐意；神是我们的避难所。　细拉
  • 62:9　Men of low degree are but a vapor; Men of high degree, a lie. In the balances they go up; Together they are less than a vapor.
  • 下流人不过是虚空，上流人不过是虚假；放在天平里就必升起；他们一共比空气还轻。
  • 62:10　Do not trust in oppression, And do not vainly hope in robbery; If wealth increases, Do not set your heart on it.
  • 不要靠强暴得财，也不要妄想靠抢夺得利；若财宝加增，不要放在心上。
  • 62:11　Once has God spoken; Twice have I heard this, That strength belongs to God.
  • 神说了一次、两次，我都听见，就是能力属于神。
  • 62:12　To You also, O Lord, belongs lovingkindness; For You repay every man according to his work.
  • 主阿，慈爱也是属于你，因为你照着各人所行的报应他。
