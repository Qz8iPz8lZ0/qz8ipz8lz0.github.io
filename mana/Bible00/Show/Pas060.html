诗篇-Psalms 60

Settings

  • 60:0
  • 大卫与两河间的亚兰并琐巴的亚兰争战的时候，约押转回，在盐谷攻击以东，杀了一万二千人；那时大卫作这金诗，叫人学习；交与歌咏长，调用见证的百合花。
  • 60:1　O God, You have rejected us; You have broken out upon us; You have been angry. Restore us.
  • 神阿，你丢弃了我们，使我们破败；你向我们发怒。求你使我们复兴。
  • 60:2　You have caused the earth to quake; You have split it open. Heal the breaks in it, for it totters.
  • 你使地震动，而且崩裂。求你将裂口医好，因为地摇动。
  • 60:3　You have shown Your people hard things; You have made us drink wine to stagger us.
  • 你叫你的民遇见艰难；你叫我们喝那使人东倒西歪的酒。
  • 60:4　You have given a banner to those who fear You, To be displayed because of the truth.
  • 你把旌旗赐给敬畏你的人，可以为真理扬起来。　细拉
  • 60:5　That Your beloved ones may be delivered, Save with Your right hand, and answer us.
  • 求你用右手拯救我们，应允我们，好叫你所亲爱的人得救。
  • 60:6　God has spoken in His holiness: I will exult; I will divide Shechem And measure out the Valley of Succoth.
  • 神已经在祂的圣别中说，我要欢乐；我要分开示剑，丈量疏割谷。
  • 60:7　Gilead is Mine, and Manasseh is Mine, And Ephraim is the helmet for My head; Judah is My scepter.
  • 基列是我的，玛拿西也是我的，以法莲是我的头盔；犹大是我的杖；
  • 60:8　Moab is My washbowl; Upon Edom I will cast My sandal; Philistia, shout aloud because of Me.
  • 摩押是我的沐浴盆；我要向以东抛鞋；非利士阿，你要因我欢呼。
  • 60:9　Who will bring me into the besieged city? Who will lead me to Edom?
  • 谁能领我进被围困的城？谁能引我到以东地？
  • 60:10　Will not You, O God, who rejected us? And will You not go forth, O God, with our armies?
  • 神阿，你不是丢弃了我们么？神阿，你不和我们的军兵一同出战么？
  • 60:11　Give us help from the adversary, For a salvation by man is vain.
  • 求你帮助我们脱离敌人，因为人的拯救是枉然的。
  • 60:12　In God we will do valiantly, And it is He who will tread down our adversaries.
  • 我们因神才得奋勇行事，践踏我们敌人的就是祂。
