诗篇-Psalms 44

Settings

  • 44:0
  • 可拉子孙的训诲诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 44:1　O God, we have heard with our ears; Our fathers have recounted it to us, The work You did in their days, In the days of old.
  • 神阿，你在古时，在我们列祖的日子所行的事，我们的列祖给我们讲述过，我们亲耳听见了。
  • 44:2　You, by Your hand, Have dispossessed nations, but You have planted our fathers; You afflicted peoples, but You spread our fathers forth.
  • 你曾用手赶出外邦人，却栽培了我们列祖；你苦待众民，却叫我们列祖发旺伸展。
  • 44:3　For they did not possess the land by their sword, Nor did their arm save them; Rather it was Your right hand and Your arm, And the light of Your countenance; For You delighted in them.
  • 因为他们不是靠自己的刀剑得地土，也不是靠自己的膀臂得拯救；乃是靠你的右手、你的膀臂、和你脸上的亮光，因为你喜悦他们。
  • 44:4　You are my King, O God; Command salvation for Jacob.
  • 神阿，你是我的王；求你出令，使雅各得拯救。
  • 44:5　Through You we thrust back our adversaries; Through Your name we trample those who rise up against us.
  • 我们藉你要推倒我们的敌人；藉你的名要践踏那起来攻击我们的人。
  • 44:6　For I will not trust in my bow, Nor will my sword save me.
  • 因为我必不倚靠我的弓，我的刀也不能拯救我。
  • 44:7　But You save us from our adversaries, And You put to shame those who hate us.
  • 惟你救了我们脱离敌人，使恨我们的人羞愧。
  • 44:8　In God we boast all day long, And we praise Your name forever.
  • 我们终日因神夸耀，还要永远赞美你的名。　细拉
  • 44:9　Nevertheless You have rejected us and humiliated us, And You have not gone forth with our armies.
  • 然而你丢弃了我们，使我们受辱，不和我们的军兵同去。
  • 44:10　You have caused us to turn back from the adversary, And those who hate us take spoil for themselves.
  • 你使我们从敌人面前转身退后；那恨我们的人任意抢夺。
  • 44:11　You have given us over like sheep to be eaten And have scattered us among the nations.
  • 你把我们交给人，好像快要被吃的羊，把我们分散在列国中。
  • 44:12　You have sold Your people for nothing And have not gone high on their price.
  • 你以贱价卖了你的子民，所得的价值并没有使你得利。
  • 44:13　You have made us a reproach to our neighbors, A mocking and a derision to those around us.
  • 你使我们受邻国的羞辱，被四围的人嗤笑讥刺。
  • 44:14　You have made us a proverb among the nations, Something to shake the head at among the peoples.
  • 你使我们在列国中成了笑谈，使众民向我们摇头。
  • 44:15　All day long my humiliation is before me, And the shame of my face covers me,
  • 我的凌辱终日在我面前，我脸上的羞愧将我遮蔽；
  • 44:16　Because of the voice of him who reproaches and reviles, Because of the presence of the enemy and of him who avenges himself.
  • 都因那辱骂并毁谤者的声音，又因仇敌和报仇者的嘴脸。
  • 44:17　All this has come upon us; but we have not forgotten You, Nor have we been false to Your covenant.
  • 这都临到我们身上；我们却没有忘记你，也没有对你的约不忠实。
  • 44:18　Our heart has not turned back, Nor have our steps deviated from Your way;
  • 我们的心没有退后，我们的脚步也没有偏离你的路；
  • 44:19　But You have crushed us in the place of jackals And have covered us with the shadow of death.
  • 但你在野狗之处压伤我们，用死荫遮蔽我们。
  • 44:20　If we have forgotten the name of our God Or spread out our hands to a strange god,
  • 倘若我们忘了我们神的名，或向别神伸开双手祷告，
  • 44:21　Will God not search this out? For He knows the hidden things of the heart.
  • 神岂不鉴察这事么？因为祂晓得人心里的隐秘。
  • 44:22　But for Your sake we are slain all day long; We are considered as sheep for slaughter.
  • 但我们为你的缘故，终日被杀，人看我们如将宰的羊。
  • 44:23　Awake! Why are You sleeping, O Lord? Arise! Do not reject us forever.
  • 主阿，求你醒起！为何尽睡呢？求你兴起！不要永远丢弃我们。
  • 44:24　Why do You hide Your face And forget our affliction and our oppression?
  • 你为何掩面，忘了我们所遭的苦难，和所受的欺压？
  • 44:25　For our soul bows down to the dust; Our body clings to the earth.
  • 我们的性命伏于尘土；我们的肚腹紧贴地面。
  • 44:26　Rise up; be our help, And redeem us for Your lovingkindness' sake.
  • 求你起来帮助我们，因你慈爱的缘故救赎我们。
