诗篇-Psalms 5

Settings

  • 5:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长；用吹的乐器。
  • 5:1　To my words give ear, O Jehovah; Consider my moaning.
  • 耶和华阿，求你侧耳听我的言语，顾念我的呻吟。
  • 5:2　Give heed to the sound of my cry, My King and my God; For to You do I pray.
  • 我的王我的神阿，求你垂听我呼求的声音；因为我向你祷告。
  • 5:3　O Jehovah, in the morning You will hear my voice; In the morning I will set forth my words in order to You, And I will watch.
  • 耶和华阿，早晨你必听我的声音；早晨我必向你陈明我的心意，并要守望。
  • 5:4　For You are not a God who delights in wickedness; Evil will not sojourn with You.
  • 因为你不是喜悦邪恶的神；恶人不能与你同居。
  • 5:5　Those who boast will not stand Before Your eyes; You hate all workers of iniquity.
  • 夸口的人不能站在你眼前；凡作孽的，都是你所恨恶的。
  • 5:6　You will destroy those who speak lies. Jehovah abhors a man of bloodshed and deceit.
  • 说谎言的，你必灭绝；好流人血弄诡诈的人，为耶和华所憎恶。
  • 5:7　But as for me, in the abundance of Your lovingkindness I will come into Your house; I will bow down toward Your holy temple In fear of You.
  • 至于我，我必凭你丰盛的慈爱进入你的居所；我必存敬畏你的心向你的圣殿下拜。
  • 5:8　O Jehovah, lead me in Your righteousness On account of those who lie in wait for me; Make Your way straight before me.
  • 耶和华阿，求你因那埋伏等候我的人，凭你的公义引领我；使你的道路在我面前平直。
  • 5:9　For there is no truth in their mouth; Their inward part is corruption itself; Their throat is an open grave; They flatter with their tongue.
  • 因为他们口中没有诚实；他们内里尽是败坏；他们的喉咙是敞开的坟墓；他们用舌头谄媚人。
  • 5:10　Hold them guilty, O God; Let them fall by their own counsels; Because of the multitude of their transgressions thrust them out, For they have rebelled against You.
  • 神阿，求你定他们有罪；愿他们因自己的计谋跌倒；愿你因他们许多的过犯，把他们赶逐出去，因为他们背叛了你。
  • 5:11　But let all who take refuge in You rejoice; Let them shout for joy forever; And may You spread a cover over them; And those who love Your name Will exult in You.
  • 凡投奔于你的，愿他们喜乐，永远欢呼；愿你覆庇他们；又愿那爱你名的人，都因你欢欣。
  • 5:12　For You will bless the righteous man, O Jehovah; You will surround him with favor as with a shield.
  • 因为耶和华阿，你必赐福与义人；你必用恩惠如同盾牌环绕他。
