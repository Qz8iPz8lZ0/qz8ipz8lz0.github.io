诗篇-Psalms 91

Settings

  • 91:1　He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High Will abide in the shadow of the Almighty.
  • 住在至高者隐密处的，必住在全能者的荫下。
  • 91:2　I say of Jehovah, My refuge and My fortress, My God in whom I trust!
  • 我要论到耶和华说，祂是我的避难所，是我的山寨，是我的神，是我所信靠的！
  • 91:3　For He will deliver You From the snare of the fowler, From the deadly pestilence.
  • 因为祂必救你脱离捕鸟人的网罗，脱离毒害的瘟疫。
  • 91:4　With His pinions He will cover You, And under His wings You will take refuge; His truth is a shield and a buckler.
  • 祂必用自己的翎毛遮蔽你，你要投靠在祂的翅膀底下；祂的真实，是大小的盾牌。
  • 91:5　You will not be afraid of the terror by night, Or of the arrow that flies by day,
  • 你必不怕黑夜的惊骇，或是白日的飞箭；
  • 91:6　Or of the pestilence that walks in darkness, Or of the destruction that lays waste at noon.
  • 也不怕黑暗中流行的瘟疫，或是午间损毁人的毒病。
  • 91:7　A thousand will fall at Your side, And ten thousand at Your right hand; But it will not come near to You.
  • 虽有千人仆倒在你旁边，万人仆倒在你右边，灾害却不得临近你。
  • 91:8　You will only look on with Your eyes And see the recompense of the wicked.
  • 不过你要亲眼观看，见恶人遭报。
  • 91:9　For You have made Jehovah, who is my refuge, Even the Most High, Your habitation,
  • 因你已将至高者，耶和华我的避难所，当你的居所；
  • 91:10　No evil will befall You, Nor will any plague come near Your tent.
  • 祸患必不临到你，灾害也不挨近你的帐棚。
  • 91:11　For He will give His angels charge concerning You To keep You in all Your ways.
  • 因祂要为你吩咐祂的使者，在你所行的一切道路上保护你。
  • 91:12　They will bear You up in their hands, Lest You dash Your foot against a stone.
  • 他们要用手托着你，免得你的脚碰在石头上。
  • 91:13　You will tread upon the lion and the cobra; You will trample the young lion and the serpent.
  • 你要踹在狮子和虺蛇的身上，践踏少壮狮子和大蛇。
  • 91:14　Because He has set His love upon Me, I will rescue Him; I will set Him on high, because He has known My name.
  • 神说，因为祂专一爱我，我要搭救祂；因为祂认识我的名，我要把祂安置在高处。
  • 91:15　He will call upon Me, and I will answer Him. I will be with Him in distress; I will deliver Him and honor Him.
  • 祂要呼求我，我就应允祂。祂在急难中，我要与祂同在；我要搭救祂，使祂尊贵。
  • 91:16　With an extension of His days I will satisfy Him, And I will show Him My salvation.
  • 我要使祂足享长寿，将我的救恩显明给祂。
