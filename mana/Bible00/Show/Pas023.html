诗篇-Psalms 23

Settings

  • 23:0
  • 大卫的诗。
  • 23:1　Jehovah is my Shepherd; I will lack nothing.
  • 耶和华是我的牧者；我必不至缺乏。
  • 23:2　He makes me lie down in green pastures; He leads me beside waters of rest.
  • 祂使我躺卧在青草地上，领我在可安歇的水边。
  • 23:3　He restores my soul; He guides me on the paths of righteousness For His name's sake.
  • 祂使我的魂苏醒，为自己的名引导我走义路。
  • 23:4　Even though I walk Through the valley of the shadow of death, I do not fear evil, For You are with me; Your rod and Your staff, They comfort me.
  • 我虽然行过死荫的幽谷，也不怕遭害，因为你与我同在；你的杖，你的竿，都安慰我。
  • 23:5　You spread a table before me In the presence of my adversaries; You anoint my head with oil; My cup runs over.
  • 在我敌人面前，你为我摆设筵席；你用油膏了我的头，使我的福杯满溢。
  • 23:6　Surely goodness and lovingkindness will follow me All the days of my life, And I will dwell in the house of Jehovah For the length of my days.
  • 我一生一世必有恩惠慈爱随着我，我且要住在耶和华的殿中，直到永远。
