诗篇-Psalms 148

Settings

  • 148:1　Hallelujah! Praise Jehovah from the heavens; Praise Him in the heights.
  • 阿利路亚！你们要从天上赞美耶和华，要在高处赞美祂。
  • 148:2　Praise Him, all His angels; Praise Him, all His hosts.
  • 祂的众使者都要赞美祂；祂的众军都要赞美祂。
  • 148:3　Praise Him, sun and moon; Praise Him, all stars of light.
  • 日头月亮，你们要赞美祂；放光的众星，你们都要赞美祂。
  • 148:4　Praise Him, you heaven of heavens, And you waters that are above the heavens.
  • 诸天的天，和诸天之上的水，你们都要赞美祂。
  • 148:5　Let them praise the name of Jehovah; For He commanded, and they were created.
  • 愿这些都赞美耶和华的名；因祂一吩咐，这些便都造成。
  • 148:6　And He established them forever and ever; He gave the decree which shall not pass away.
  • 祂将这些立定，直到永永远远；祂颁布了命令，就不能废去。
  • 148:7　Praise Jehovah from the earth, You sea monsters and all the depths;
  • 你们要从地上赞美耶和华，大鱼和一切深渊，
  • 148:8　Fire and hail, snow and smoke; Stormy wind that executes His word;
  • 火与冰雹，雪和烟雾，执行祂话的狂风，
  • 148:9　Mountains and all hills; Fruit trees and all cedars;
  • 大山和一切小山，结果子的树木和一切香柏树，
  • 148:10　Animals wild and tame; Creeping things and birds of flight;
  • 野兽和一切牲畜，爬物和飞鸟，
  • 148:11　Kings of the earth and all peoples; Princes and all judges of the earth;
  • 地上的君王和万民，首领和地上一切审判官，
  • 148:12　Choice young men as well as virgins; Old men with young men.
  • 少年人以及处女，老年人并孩童，都当赞美耶和华。
  • 148:13　Let them praise the name of Jehovah, For His name alone is exalted; His glory is above the earth and the heavens.
  • 愿这些都赞美耶和华的名，因为独有祂的名被尊崇；祂的荣美超乎天地之上。
  • 148:14　And He has raised up a horn for His people, The praise of all His faithful ones, The children of Israel, a people near to Him. Hallelujah!
  • 祂已将祂百姓的角举起，使祂一切圣民以色列人，就是与祂相近的百姓，都赞美祂。阿利路亚！
