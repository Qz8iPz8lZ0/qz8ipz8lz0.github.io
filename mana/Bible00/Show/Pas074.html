诗篇-Psalms 74

Settings

  • 74:0
  • 亚萨的训诲诗。
  • 74:1　Why, O God, have You cast us off forever? Why does Your anger smoke against the sheep of Your pasture?
  • 神阿，你为何永远丢弃我们呢？你的怒气为何向你草场的羊如烟冒出呢？
  • 74:2　Remember Your assembly, which You have purchased of old, Which You have redeemed as the tribe of Your inheritance, And Mount Zion, where You dwell.
  • 求你记念你古时所买来的会众，就是你所赎作你产业支派的，并记念你所居住的锡安山。
  • 74:3　Lift up Your steps unto the perpetual ruins; The enemy has damaged everything in the sanctuary.
  • 求你举步到那长久荒凉之地；仇敌在圣所中将一切尽行毁灭。
  • 74:4　Your adversaries have roared in the midst of Your assembly place; They have set up their symbols as signs.
  • 你的敌人在你会所中吼叫；他们竖了自己的旗号为标帜。
  • 74:5　It looked like when men bring up axes On the thicket of the trees.
  • 他们好像人扬起斧子，砍伐林中的树。
  • 74:6　And now they have struck down with hatchet and hammer Its carved work altogether.
  • 圣所中一切的雕刻，现在他们都用斧子、锤子打坏了。
  • 74:7　They have set fire to Your sanctuary; They have defiled the dwelling place of Your name to the ground.
  • 他们用火焚烧你的圣所，亵渎你名的住处，拆毁到地。
  • 74:8　They said in their heart, Let us oppress them completely. They have burned down all God's assembly places in the land.
  • 他们心里说，我们要把这些尽行毁灭；他们就把神在地上的会所都烧毁了。
  • 74:9　We do not see our signs; there is no longer a prophet, Nor is there anyone among us who knows how long.
  • 我们不见我们的标帜；不再有申言者；我们内中也没有人知道这灾祸要到几时。
  • 74:10　How long, O God, will the adversary reproach? Will the enemy despise Your name forever?
  • 神阿，敌人辱骂要到几时呢？仇敌藐视你的名，要到永远么？
  • 74:11　Why do You withdraw Your hand, even Your right hand? Bring it forth from the midst of Your bosom; destroy them .
  • 你为什么缩回你的手，缩回你的右手？求你从怀中伸出来，毁灭他们。
  • 74:12　But God is my King of old, Accomplishing salvation in the midst of the earth.
  • 但神自古以来为我的王，在全地中施行拯救。
  • 74:13　You divided the sea by Your strength; You broke the heads of the sea serpents on the waters.
  • 你曾用能力把海分开，将水上海蛇的头打破。
  • 74:14　You crushed the heads of the leviathan; You gave him as food to the creatures of the desert.
  • 你曾砸碎鳄鱼的头，把它给荒野的禽兽为食物。
  • 74:15　You broke open the fountain and the torrent; You dried up the ever-flowing streams.
  • 你曾开出泉源和急流；你使长流的江河干了。
  • 74:16　The day is Yours; the night also is Yours; You prepared the moon and the sun.
  • 白昼属你，黑夜也属你；月亮和日头是你所预备的。
  • 74:17　You established all the borders of the earth: You formed the summer and the winter.
  • 地的一切疆界，是你所立的；夏天和冬天是你所定的。
  • 74:18　Remember this: The enemy has reproached Jehovah, And a foolish people has despised Your name.
  • 耶和华阿，仇敌辱骂你，愚顽民藐视你的名，求你记念这事。
  • 74:19　Do not give the soul of Your turtledove to the wild beast; Do not forget the life of Your poor forever.
  • 不要将你斑鸠的性命交给野兽；不要永远忘记你穷苦人的生命。
  • 74:20　Regard the covenant, For the dark places of the earth are full of the habitations of violence.
  • 求你顾念所立的约，因为地上黑暗之处，都满了强暴的居所。
  • 74:21　Do not let him who is oppressed return ashamed. May the poor and needy praise Your name.
  • 不要叫受欺压的人蒙羞回去。愿困苦穷乏的人赞美你的名。
  • 74:22　Arise, O God; contend for Your own cause; Remember how the foolish man reproaches You all day long.
  • 神阿，求你起来，为自己伸诉；要记念愚顽人怎样终日辱骂你。
  • 74:23　Do not forget the voice of Your adversaries; The roar of those who rise up against You goes up continually.
  • 不要忘记你敌人的声音；那起来敌你之人的喧哗时常上升。
