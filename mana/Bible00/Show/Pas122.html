诗篇-Psalms 122

Settings

  • 122:0
  • 大卫的上行之歌。
  • 122:1　I rejoiced when they said to me, Let us go to the house of Jehovah.
  • 人对我说，我们往耶和华的殿去，我就欢喜。
  • 122:2　Our feet are standing Within your gates, O Jerusalem.
  • 耶路撒冷阿，我们的脚，站在你的门内。
  • 122:3　Jerusalem has been built as a city That has been compacted together;
  • 耶路撒冷被建造，如同连络整齐的一座城；
  • 122:4　To which the tribes go up, The tribes of Jehovah-A custom for Israel-To give thanks to the name of Jehovah.
  • 众支派，就是耶和华的支派，上那里去，作以色列的见证，称谢耶和华的名。
  • 122:5　For there thrones are set for judgment, Thrones of the house of David.
  • 因为在那里设立了审判的宝座，就是大卫家的宝座。
  • 122:6　Pray for the peace of Jerusalem. Those who love you will prosper.
  • 你们要为耶路撒冷求平安。耶路撒冷阿，爱你的人必然兴旺。
  • 122:7　Peace be within your bulwarks, And prosperity within your citadels.
  • 愿你城中平安，愿你宫内兴旺。
  • 122:8　For my brothers' and companions' sake I will now say, Peace be within you.
  • 因我弟兄和同伴的缘故，我要说，愿平安在你中间。
  • 122:9　For the sake of the house of Jehovah our God I will seek your good.
  • 因耶和华我们神殿的缘故，我要为你求好处。
