诗篇-Psalms 57

Settings

  • 57:0
  • 大卫逃避扫罗，藏在洞里；那时他作了这金诗，交与歌咏长；调用休要毁坏。
  • 57:1　Be gracious to me, O God, be gracious to me; For my soul takes refuge in You; Indeed in the shadow of Your wings I will take refuge, Until the destruction passes by.
  • 神阿，求你恩待我，恩待我；因为我的心投靠你；我要投靠在你翅膀的荫下，等到灾害过去。
  • 57:2　I call out to God the Most High, To the God who accomplishes all things for me.
  • 我要呼求至高的神，就是为我成全诸事的神。
  • 57:3　He sends forth from heaven and saves me; He reproaches him who would trample me; God sends forth His lovingkindness and His faithfulness.
  • 神必从天上发出慈爱和信实，并拯救我，祂斥责那践踏我的人；细拉神必向我发出祂的慈爱和信实。
  • 57:4　My soul is among lions; I lie down among those who blaze forth, the sons of men, Whose teeth are spears and arrows, And whose tongue is a sharp sword.
  • 我的性命在狮子中间；我躺卧在性如烈火的世人当中，他们的牙齿是枪是箭；他们的舌头是快刀。
  • 57:5　Be exalted above the heavens, O God; Let Your glory be above all the earth.
  • 神阿，愿你被高举，过于诸天；愿你的荣耀遍及全地。
  • 57:6　They have prepared a net for my steps; My soul is bowed down. They have dug a pit before me; They have fallen into it themselves.
  • 他们为我的脚步设下网罗，压制我的心。他们在我面前挖了坑，自己反掉在其中。　细拉
  • 57:7　My heart is steadfast, O God; My heart is steadfast. I will sing, indeed I will sing psalms.
  • 神阿，我心坚定，我心坚定。我要唱诗，我要歌颂。
  • 57:8　Awake, my glory; Awake, O harp and lyre! I will awaken the dawn.
  • 我的荣耀阿，你当醒起；琴瑟阿，你们当醒起！我要极早醒起。
  • 57:9　I will give thanks to You among the peoples, O Lord; I will sing psalms to you among the nations.
  • 主阿，我要在众民中称谢你；我要在列邦中歌颂你。
  • 57:10　For Your lovingkindness is as great as the heavens; And Your faithfulness reaches the sky.
  • 因为你的慈爱如诸天之大；你的信实达到穹苍。
  • 57:11　Be exalted above the heavens, O God; Let Your glory be above all the earth.
  • 神阿，愿你被高举，过于诸天；愿你的荣耀遍及全地。
