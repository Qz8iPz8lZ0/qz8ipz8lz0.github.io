诗篇-Psalms 89

Settings

  • 89:0
  • 以斯拉人以探的训诲诗。
  • 89:1　I will sing of the lovingkindness of Jehovah forever; From generation to generation I will make known Your faithfulness with my mouth.
  • 我要歌唱耶和华的慈爱，直到永远；我要用口将你的信实传与万代。
  • 89:2　For I have said, Lovingkindness will be built up forever; In the heavens themselves You will establish Your faithfulness.
  • 因我曾说，你的慈爱必建立到永远；你必将你的信实，坚立在诸天之上。
  • 89:3　I have made a covenant with My chosen one; I have sworn to David My servant:
  • 我与我所拣选的人立了约；我向我的仆人大卫起了誓：
  • 89:4　I will establish your seed forever, And I will build up your throne from generation to generation.
  • 我要坚立你的后裔，直到永远；我要建立你的宝座，直到万代。　细拉
  • 89:5　And the heavens will praise Your wonders, O Jehovah, Indeed Your faithfulness in the congregation of the holy ones.
  • 耶和华阿，诸天要称赞你的奇事；在圣者的会中，要称赞你的信实。
  • 89:6　For who in the skies can be compared to Jehovah? Who among the sons of the mighty is like Jehovah,
  • 在天空谁能比耶和华呢？大能者的众子中，有谁像耶和华呢？
  • 89:7　Like God, greatly feared in the council of the holy ones And awesome beyond all around Him?
  • 祂在圣者的议会中，是大可畏的神，比一切在祂四围的更可畏惧。
  • 89:8　O Jehovah God of hosts, who is like You, the mighty Jah? Your faithfulness also surrounds You.
  • 耶和华万军之神阿，谁能像你这大能的耶和华呢？你的信实是在你的四围。
  • 89:9　You rule the swelling of the sea: When its waves rise up, You still them.
  • 你管辖海的狂涛：波浪翻腾，你使它平静。
  • 89:10　You crushed Rahab like one slain; You scattered Your enemies with the arm of Your strength.
  • 你把拉哈伯打碎，如同被杀的人；你用有能的膀臂打散了你的仇敌。
  • 89:11　The heavens are Yours; the earth also is Yours; The world and its fullness You have founded.
  • 诸天属你，地也属你；世界和其中所充满的，都是你建立的。
  • 89:12　You created the north and the south; Tabor and Hermon shout for joy at Your name.
  • 南北都是你创造的；他泊和黑门都因你的名欢呼。
  • 89:13　You have an arm with might; Your hand is strong; Your right hand is high.
  • 你有大能的膀臂；你的手有力，你的右手也高举。
  • 89:14　Righteousness and justice are the foundation of Your throne; Lovingkindness and truth go before Your face.
  • 公义和公平，是你宝座的根基；慈爱和真实，行在你面前。
  • 89:15　Blessed are the people who know the joyful shout; They walk, O Jehovah, in the light of Your countenance.
  • 知道向你欢呼的，那民是有福的；耶和华阿，他们在你的面光中行走。
  • 89:16　In Your name they exult all day long, And in Your righteousness they are exalted.
  • 他们因你的名终日欢腾，因你的公义得以高举。
  • 89:17　For You are the glory of their strength, And in Your favor our horn is exalted.
  • 你是他们力量的荣耀；因着你的恩惠，我们的角必被高举。
  • 89:18　For our shield belongs to Jehovah, And our king, to the Holy One of Israel.
  • 我们的盾牌属耶和华，我们的王属以色列的圣者。
  • 89:19　Then You spoke in vision Of Your Holy One, and You said, I have given help to One who is mighty; I have exalted One chosen from My people.
  • 当时你在异象中论到你的圣者，说，我已把救助之力，加给那有能者；我已高举那从民中所拣选的。
  • 89:20　I have found David My Servant; With My holy oil I have anointed Him,
  • 我寻得我的仆人大卫，用我的圣膏油膏了祂。
  • 89:21　With whom My hand will be established; My arm will also strengthen Him.
  • 我的手必坚定的与祂同在；我的膀臂也必坚固祂。
  • 89:22　The enemy will not exact anything from Him, Nor will the son of wickedness afflict Him.
  • 仇敌必不勒索祂，凶恶之子也不苦害祂。
  • 89:23　And I will crush His adversaries before Him, And I will strike those who hate Him.
  • 我要在祂面前打碎祂的敌人，击败那些恨祂的人。
  • 89:24　And My faithfulness and My lovingkindness will be with Him, And in My name His horn will be exalted.
  • 我的信实和我的慈爱，要与祂同在；因我的名，祂的角必被高举。
  • 89:25　And I will set His hand on the sea And His right hand on the rivers.
  • 我要使祂的左手伸到海上，右手伸到河上。
  • 89:26　He will call upon Me, saying, You are My Father, My God and the rock of My salvation.
  • 祂要称呼我说，你是我的父，是我的神，是拯救我的磐石。
  • 89:27　I will also make Him the Firstborn, The highest of the kings of the earth.
  • 我也要立祂为长子，为地上最高的君王。
  • 89:28　I will keep My lovingkindness for Him forever, And My covenant will stand firm with Him.
  • 我要向祂守住我的慈爱，直到永远；我与祂立的约，必要坚定。
  • 89:29　And I will establish His seed forever, And His throne as the days of heaven.
  • 我也要使祂的后裔存到永远，使祂的宝座如天之久。
  • 89:30　If His children forsake My law And do not walk in My ordinances,
  • 倘若祂的子孙离弃我的律法，不遵行我的典章，
  • 89:31　If they profane My statutes And do not keep My commandments,
  • 渎犯我的律例，不遵守我的诫命，
  • 89:32　I will punish their transgression with a rod And their iniquity with stripes.
  • 我就要用杖责罚他们的过犯，用鞭责罚他们的罪孽。
  • 89:33　But My lovingkindness I will not utterly take away from Him, Nor will I be false to My own faithfulness.
  • 只是我必不将我的慈爱，全然从祂挪去，也必不背弃我的信实。
  • 89:34　I will not profane My covenant, Nor will I change what has gone forth from My lips.
  • 我必不违犯我的约，也不改变我嘴唇所出的。
  • 89:35　Once I have sworn by My holiness; I will not lie to David.
  • 我一次指着自己的圣别起誓；我必不向大卫说谎。
  • 89:36　His seed shall endure forever, And His throne, like the sun before Me;
  • 祂的后裔要存到永远，祂的宝座在我面前，如日之恒一般；
  • 89:37　It shall be established forever like the moon; And the witness in the skies is firm.
  • 又如月亮永远坚立；这天上的见证是确实的。　细拉
  • 89:38　But You have cast off and rejected; You have been angry with Your anointed.
  • 但你已丢掉弃绝他；你已恼怒你的受膏者。
  • 89:39　You have abhorred the covenant of Your servant; You have profaned his crown by casting it to the ground.
  • 你厌恶了与你仆人所立的约，将他的冠冕抛掷于地，玷污了。
  • 89:40　You have broken down all his walls; You have brought his strongholds to ruin.
  • 你拆毁了他一切的围墙；你使他的保障变为荒场。
  • 89:41　All who pass by on the way plunder him; He has become a reproach to his neighbors.
  • 凡过路的人都抢夺他；他成了邻国所羞辱的。
  • 89:42　You have exalted the right hand of his adversaries; You have caused all his enemies to rejoice.
  • 你高举了他敌人的右手；你叫他一切的仇敌喜乐。
  • 89:43　Indeed You have turned back the edge of his sword And have not made him stand in the battle.
  • 你叫他的刀剑卷刃，叫他在争战之中站立不住。
  • 89:44　You have made his brightness cease And have cast down his throne to the ground.
  • 你使他的光辉止息，将他的宝座推倒于地。
  • 89:45　You have shortened the days of his youth; You have covered him with shame.
  • 你减少他青年的日子；你使他披上羞愧。　细拉
  • 89:46　How long, O Jehovah? Will You hide Yourself forever? How long will Your wrath burn like fire?
  • 耶和华阿，这要到几时呢？你要将自己隐藏到永远么？你的忿怒如火焚烧，要到几时呢？
  • 89:47　Remember how short my time is-For what vanity You have created all the sons of men!
  • 求你记念我的时候是何等的短少－你所创造的一切世人，你要使他们归于何等的虚空！
  • 89:48　What man will live and not see death? Will he deliver his soul from the power of Sheol?
  • 什么人能常活而不见死？他能救自己的魂脱离阴间的权势么？　细拉
  • 89:49　Where are Your former acts of lovingkindness, O Lord, Which You swore to David in Your faithfulness?
  • 主阿，你先前所施行的慈爱，就是你凭自己的信实向大卫起誓的，在那里呢？
  • 89:50　Remember, O Lord, the reproach of Your servants, How I bear in my bosom the reproach of all the mighty peoples,
  • 主阿，求你记念仆人们所受的羞辱，记念我怎样将一切强盛之民的羞辱承当在我怀里；
  • 89:51　With which Your enemies have reproached, O Jehovah, With which they have reproached the footsteps of Your anointed.
  • 耶和华阿，你的仇敌，用这羞辱羞辱了你的仆人，羞辱了你受膏者的脚踪。
  • 89:52　Blessed be Jehovah forever. Amen and Amen.
  • 耶和华是当受颂赞的，直到永远。阿们，阿们。
