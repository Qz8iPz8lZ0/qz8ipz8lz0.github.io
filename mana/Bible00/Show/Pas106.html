诗篇-Psalms 106

Settings

  • 106:1　Hallelujah. Give thanks to Jehovah, for He is good, For His lovingkindness is forever.
  • 阿利路亚。你们要称谢耶和华，因祂本为善，祂的慈爱永远长存。
  • 106:2　Who can utter the mighty acts of Jehovah, Or declare all His praise?
  • 谁能述说耶和华大能的作为？谁能宣告一切赞美祂的话？
  • 106:3　Blessed are those who maintain justice, Who execute righteousness at all times.
  • 那些维护公理，常行公义的人，是有福的。
  • 106:4　Remember me, O Jehovah, with Your favor toward Your people; Visit me with Your salvation,
  • 耶和华阿，你用恩惠待你的百姓，求你也用这恩惠记念我；求你用你的救恩眷顾我，
  • 106:5　That I may see the prosperity of Your chosen ones, That I may rejoice in the joy of Your nation, That I may glory with Your inheritance.
  • 使我得见你的选民享美福，因你国民的喜乐而喜乐，与你的产业一同夸耀。
  • 106:6　We have sinned with our fathers; We committed iniquity; we have acted wickedly.
  • 我们与我们的祖宗一同犯罪；我们作了孽，行了恶。
  • 106:7　Our fathers in Egypt Did not understand Your wonders; They did not remember the multitude of Your acts of lovingkindness, But rebelled by the sea, at the Red Sea.
  • 我们的祖宗在埃及不明白你的奇事；他们不记念你丰盛的慈爱，反倒在红海边行了悖逆。
  • 106:8　But He saved them for His name's sake, That He might make His power known.
  • 然而祂因自己的名拯救他们，为要使人知道祂的大能。
  • 106:9　Thus He rebuked the Red Sea, and it dried up; And He led them through the depths as through the wilderness.
  • 祂斥责红海，海便干了；祂带领他们经过深处，如同经过旷野。
  • 106:10　And He saved them from the hand of him who hated them, And redeemed them from the hand of the enemy.
  • 祂拯救他们脱离恨他们之人的手，救赎他们脱离仇敌的手。
  • 106:11　The water then covered their adversaries; Not one of them was left.
  • 水淹没他们的敌人，没有一个存留。
  • 106:12　Then they believed His words; They sang His praise.
  • 那时他们才信了祂的话，歌唱赞美祂。
  • 106:13　Soon they forgot His works; They did not wait for His counsel,
  • 他们很快就忘了祂的作为，不等候祂的指教，
  • 106:14　But lusted exceedingly in the wilderness, And tempted God in the desert.
  • 反倒在旷野大起贪欲，在荒地试探神。
  • 106:15　And He gave them their request, But He sent leanness into their soul.
  • 祂将他们所求的赐给他们，却使他们的魂衰弱。
  • 106:16　And they were jealous of Moses in the camp And of Aaron, the holy one of Jehovah.
  • 他们又在营中嫉妒摩西，和耶和华的圣者亚伦。
  • 106:17　The earth opened, and it swallowed up Dathan And covered the company of Abiram.
  • 于是地裂开，吞下大坍，并掩盖亚比兰一党的人。
  • 106:18　And a fire burned among their company; A flame engulfed the wicked.
  • 有火在他们的党中着起，有火焰烧毁了恶人。
  • 106:19　They made a calf in Horeb, And worshipped a molten image.
  • 他们在何烈山造了牛犊，叩拜铸成的像。
  • 106:20　Thus they exchanged their glory For the image of an ox that eats grass.
  • 如此将他们的荣耀，换为吃草之牛的像。
  • 106:21　They forgot God their Savior, Who had done great things in Egypt,
  • 他们竟忘了神他们的救主；祂曾在埃及行大事，
  • 106:22　Wondrous deeds in the land of Ham, Awesome things by the Red Sea.
  • 在含地行奇事，在红海边行可畏的事。
  • 106:23　Therefore He said that He would destroy them, And would have, had not Moses His chosen one Stood in the breach before Him To turn away His wrath from destroying them.
  • 所以祂说要灭绝他们；若非有祂所拣选的摩西站在祂面前，堵住破口，使祂的忿怒转消，不灭绝他们，他们早已灭亡了。
  • 106:24　Then they despised the pleasant land; They did not believe in His word;
  • 他们又藐视那可喜爱之地，不信祂的话；
  • 106:25　Rather they murmured in their tents; They did not listen to the voice of Jehovah.
  • 反倒在自己帐棚内发怨言，不听耶和华的声音。
  • 106:26　Therefore He lifted up His hand and swore to them That He would make them fall in the wilderness,
  • 所以祂对他们举手起誓，必叫他们倒在旷野，
  • 106:27　And that He would make their seed fall among the nations, And that He would disperse them among the lands.
  • 叫他们的后裔倒在列国之中，分散在各地。
  • 106:28　Then they joined themselves to Baal-peor, And ate sacrifices offered to the dead.
  • 他们又与巴力毗珥连合，且吃了祭死神的物。
  • 106:29　Thus they provoked Him to anger by their actions, And the plague broke out among them.
  • 这样，他们以所行的惹耶和华发怒，便有瘟疫爆发在他们中间。
  • 106:30　Then Phinehas stood up and intervened, And the plague was stayed;
  • 那时非尼哈站起来干豫，瘟疫才止息。
  • 106:31　And it has been accounted to him as righteousness From generation to generation unto eternity.
  • 那就算为他的义，世世代代直到永远。
  • 106:32　Then they angered Him at the waters of Meribah, So that it went ill with Moses on account of them;
  • 他们在米利巴水又叫耶和华发怒，甚至摩西也因他们受了亏损；
  • 106:33　For they rebelled against His Spirit, And he spoke rashly with his lips.
  • 是因他们违背祂的灵；摩西用嘴说了急躁的话。
  • 106:34　They did not destroy the peoples, As Jehovah had commanded them;
  • 他们不照耶和华所吩咐的，灭绝外邦民，
  • 106:35　But they mingled themselves with the nations And learned their practices.
  • 反与他们混杂相合，学习他们的行为，
  • 106:36　And they served their idols, Which became a snare to them.
  • 事奉他们的偶像；这就成了自己的网罗。
  • 106:37　They also sacrificed their sons And their daughters to demons,
  • 他们又把自己的儿女献给鬼魔为祭物，
  • 106:38　And poured out innocent blood, The blood of their sons and daughters, whom they sacrificed to the idols of Canaan; And the land was polluted with bloodshed.
  • 流无辜人的血，就是自己儿女的血，把他们献给迦南的偶像为祭物；那地就被血污秽了。
  • 106:39　Thus they were defiled by their practices, And went about as harlots by their actions.
  • 这样，他们被自己所作的污秽了，在行为上犯了邪淫。
  • 106:40　And the anger of Jehovah was kindled against His people, And He abhorred His inheritance.
  • 耶和华向祂的百姓发怒，憎恶祂的产业。
  • 106:41　And He gave them over into the hand of the nations: And those who hated them ruled over them,
  • 祂将他们交在外邦人的手里：恨他们的人就辖制他们，
  • 106:42　And their enemies oppressed them, And they were subdued under their hand.
  • 他们的仇敌也欺压他们，他们就伏在那些人手下。
  • 106:43　Many times He delivered them, But they were rebellious in their counsel, And they were brought low in their iniquity.
  • 祂屡次搭救他们，他们却设谋悖逆，就因自己的罪孽降为卑下。
  • 106:44　Yet He regarded their distress When He heard their cry,
  • 然而祂听见他们呼求的时候，就看顾他们的急难；
  • 106:45　And for their sake He remembered His covenant, And repented according to the multitude of His acts of lovingkindness;
  • 为他们的缘故记念祂的约，照祂丰盛的慈爱后悔；
  • 106:46　And He caused them to find compassion With all their captors.
  • 祂也使他们在一切掳掠他们的人面前蒙怜恤。
  • 106:47　Save us, O Jehovah our God; And gather us out of the nations, That we may give thanks to Your holy name, That we may glory in Your praise.
  • 耶和华我们的神阿，求你拯救我们，从外邦中招聚我们，我们好称谢你的圣名，以赞美你为夸耀。
  • 106:48　Blessed be Jehovah the God of Israel, From eternity to eternity. And let all the people say, Amen. Hallelujah.
  • 耶和华以色列的神，从亘古直到永远，是当受颂赞的。愿众民都说，阿们。阿利路亚。
