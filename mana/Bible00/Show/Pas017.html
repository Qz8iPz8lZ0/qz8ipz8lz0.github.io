诗篇-Psalms 17

Settings

  • 17:0
  • 大卫的祷告。
  • 17:1　Hear, O Jehovah, what is righteous; Give heed to my cry; Give ear to my prayer, Which is not made with lips of deceit.
  • 耶和华阿，求你听闻公义，垂听我的呼吁；求你侧耳听我这不出于诡诈嘴唇的祷告。
  • 17:2　May a judgment for me come forth from Your presence; May Your eyes regard equity.
  • 愿你对我的判语，从你面前发出；愿你的眼睛察看公正。
  • 17:3　You have examined my heart; You have visited me by night; You have tried me: You have found nothing. I have resolved that my mouth will not transgress.
  • 你已经试验我的心；你在夜间鉴察我；你熬炼我，却找不着什么。我立志使我口中没有过犯。
  • 17:4　Regarding the works of man, by the word of Your lips, I have kept myself from the paths of the violent man.
  • 论到人的行为，我借着你嘴唇的言语，保守自己不行强暴人的道路。
  • 17:5　My steps have held fast to Your tracks; My footsteps have not slipped.
  • 我的步伐稳踏在你的路径上，我的脚步未曾滑跌。
  • 17:6　I call on You, for You will answer me, O God; Incline Your ear to me; hear my speaking.
  • 神阿，我呼求你，因为你必应允我；求你向我侧耳，听我的言语。
  • 17:7　Wondrously display Your lovingkindness, You who save by Your right hand Those who take refuge in You from them who rise up against them.
  • 求你奇妙的显示你的慈爱；你用右手拯救投靠你的，脱离起来攻击他们的人。
  • 17:8　Guard me like the pupil of Your eye; In the shadow of Your wings hide me
  • 求你保护我，如同保护你眼中的瞳人；将我隐藏在你翅膀的荫下，
  • 17:9　From the presence of the wicked who oppress me, From my deadly enemies, who close in upon me.
  • 使我脱离欺压我的恶人，就是围困我，要害我命的仇敌。
  • 17:10　They have shut up their fat hearts; With their mouths they speak arrogantly.
  • 他们关闭了他们被脂油包裹的心，用口说狂妄的话。
  • 17:11　Now they have surrounded us in our steps; They have fixed their eyes on casting us down to the ground.
  • 现在他们围困了我们的脚步；他们瞪着眼，要把我们推倒在地。
  • 17:12　He is like a lion that is eager to tear, And like a young lion lurking in hidden places.
  • 他像狮子急要抓食，又像少壮狮子蹲伏在隐密处。
  • 17:13　Arise, O Jehovah, confront him; bring him down. With Your sword rescue my soul from the wicked man,
  • 耶和华阿，求你起来，迎面抵挡他，将他打倒。求你用你的刀救护我的性命脱离恶人；
  • 17:14　From mortal men with Your hand, O Jehovah, From mortal men of the world, whose portion is in this life, And whose belly You fill with Your treasure; They are satisfied with children, And they leave their abundance for their little ones.
  • 耶和华阿，求你用手救护我脱离世人，脱离那些只在今生有业分的世人；你把你的财宝充满他们的肚腹；他们因有儿女就心满意足，将他们的富余留给他们的婴孩。
  • 17:15　As for me, in righteousness I will behold Your face; When I awake, I will be satisfied with Your likeness.
  • 至于我，我必在义中见你的面；我醒了的时候，必因见你的形像而心满意足。
