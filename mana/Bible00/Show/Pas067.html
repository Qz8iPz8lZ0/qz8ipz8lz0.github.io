诗篇-Psalms 67

Settings

  • 67:0
  • 一篇诗，一首歌，交与歌咏长；用丝弦的乐器。
  • 67:1　God be gracious to us and bless us; May He make His face to shine upon us;
  • 愿神怜悯我们，赐福与我们；愿祂用脸光照我们；　细拉
  • 67:2　That Your way may be known on earth, And Your salvation among all the nations.
  • 好叫人在地上得知你的道路，在万国中得知你的救恩。
  • 67:3　Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let the peoples praise You, all of them.
  • 神阿，愿众民赞美你；愿众民都赞美你。
  • 67:4　Let the nations rejoice and shout for joy, For You will judge the peoples equitably And will guide the nations on the earth.
  • 愿列邦喜乐欢呼，因为你必按公正审判众民，引导地上的列邦。　细拉
  • 67:5　Let the peoples praise You, O God; Let the peoples praise You, all of them.
  • 神阿，愿众民赞美你；愿众民都赞美你。
  • 67:6　The earth has yielded its increase; God, our God, blesses us.
  • 地已经出了土产；神，就是我们的神，要赐福与我们。
  • 67:7　God blesses us; And all the ends of the earth will fear Him.
  • 神要赐福与我们；地的四极都要敬畏祂。
