诗篇-Psalms 72

Settings

  • 72:0
  • 所罗门的诗。
  • 72:1　O God, give Your judgments to the king, And Your righteousness to the son of the king.
  • 神阿，求你将你的判断赐给王，将你的公义赐给王的儿子。
  • 72:2　He will judge Your people in righteousness, And Your poor with justice.
  • 祂要按公义审判你的民，按公平审判你的穷苦人。
  • 72:3　The mountains will bear peace to the people, And the hills, in righteousness.
  • 大山小山，都要在公义中给民结出平安的果子。
  • 72:4　He will judge the poor of the people; He will save the children of the needy And crush the oppressor.
  • 祂必为民中的穷苦人伸冤，拯救穷乏之辈，压碎那欺压人的。
  • 72:5　They will fear You as long as the sun endures And as long as the moon endures Throughout the generations.
  • 太阳还存，月亮还在，人要敬畏你，直到万代。
  • 72:6　He will drop like rain upon mown grass, Like abundant showers dripping on the earth.
  • 祂必降临，像雨降在已割的草地上，如甘霖浇灌大地。
  • 72:7　In His days the righteous will flourish, And there will be an abundance of peace Until the moon is no more.
  • 在祂的日子义人要发旺，大有平安，直到月亮不存。
  • 72:8　And He will have dominion from sea to sea And from the River unto the ends of the earth.
  • 祂要执掌权柄，从这海直到那海，从大河直到地极。
  • 72:9　The desert dwellers will bow down before Him, And His enemies will lick the dust.
  • 住在荒野的，必在祂面前下拜，祂的仇敌必要舔土。
  • 72:10　The kings of Tarshish and of the coastlands Will pay tribute; The kings of Sheba and Seba Will present gifts.
  • 他施和海岛并沿海一带的王要进贡；示巴和西巴的王要献礼物。
  • 72:11　And all the kings will bow down before Him; All the nations will serve Him.
  • 诸王都要叩拜祂；万国都要事奉祂。
  • 72:12　For He will deliver the needy man when he cries, And the poor man who has no one to help him.
  • 因为穷乏人呼求的时候，祂要搭救，没有人帮助的穷苦人，祂也要搭救。
  • 72:13　He will have pity on the weak and needy, And the souls of the needy He will save.
  • 祂要怜惜贫寒和穷乏的人，拯救穷乏人的性命。
  • 72:14　From oppression and violence He will redeem their soul, And their blood will be precious in His sight.
  • 祂要救赎他们的性命脱离欺压和强暴，他们的血在祂眼中看为宝贵。
  • 72:15　So He shall live and to Him shall be given The gold of Sheba; And prayer shall be made continually for Him; He shall be blessed all the day long.
  • 祂要长久活着，示巴的金子要奉给祂；人要常常为祂祷告，终日颂赞祂。
  • 72:16　There will be an abundance of grain on the earth, Even at the tops of the mountains. Its fruit will wave like the forests of Lebanon, And those of the city will blossom like the foliage of the earth.
  • 在地上，甚至在山顶上，五谷必然茂盛。所结的谷实，要摇曳如利巴嫩的树林；城里的人要发旺如地上的草。
  • 72:17　His name will be forever; As long as the sun endures, His name will spread; And men will be blessed in Him; All the nations will call Him blessed.
  • 祂的名要存到永远，要留传如日之久；人要因祂蒙福；万国要称祂有福。
  • 72:18　Blessed be Jehovah God, the God of Israel, Who alone does wondrous deeds;
  • 独行奇事的耶和华神，以色列的神，是当受颂赞的；
  • 72:19　And blessed be His glorious name forever; And may His glory fill the whole earth. Amen and Amen.
  • 祂荣耀的名也当受颂赞，直到永远；愿祂的荣耀充满全地。阿们，阿们。
  • 72:20　The prayers of David the son of Jesse are ended.
  • 耶西的儿子大卫的祷告完毕。
