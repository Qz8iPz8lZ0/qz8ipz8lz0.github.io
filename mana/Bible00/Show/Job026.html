约伯记-Job 26

Settings

  • 26:1　Then Job answered and said,
  • 约伯回答说，
  • 26:2　How you have helped him who is without power! How you have saved the arm of him who is without strength!
  • 无能的人，蒙你何等的帮助！膀臂无力的人，蒙你何等的拯救！
  • 26:3　How you have counseled him who is without wisdom And have plentifully declared sound knowledge!
  • 无智慧的人，蒙你何等的指教；你向他指示的智谋何其多！
  • 26:4　To whom have you uttered words? And whose spirit has come forth from you?
  • 你向谁发出言语？谁的灵从你而出？
  • 26:5　The deceased are made to tremble Beneath the waters, and those who inhabit them.
  • 阴魂在诸水以下战兢，住在水中的也是如此。
  • 26:6　Sheol is naked before Him, And Abaddon has no covering.
  • 在神面前阴间赤露敞开，灭亡之地也无所遮掩。
  • 26:7　He stretches out the north over the void; He hangs the earth upon nothing.
  • 神将北极铺在空中，将大地悬于虚无之上；
  • 26:8　He binds up the waters in His thick clouds, And the cloud is not rent under them.
  • 祂将水包在密云中，水下的云却不破裂。
  • 26:9　He covers the face of His throne; He spreads His cloud over it.
  • 祂遮蔽自己的宝座，将云铺在其上；
  • 26:10　He drew a circle on the surface of the waters As a boundary of the light and the darkness.
  • 在水面画出圆圈为界，作为光暗的交界。
  • 26:11　The pillars of heaven shook And were astounded at His rebuke.
  • 天的柱子因祂的斥责震动惊奇。
  • 26:12　By His power He stilled the sea, And by His understanding He struck down Rahab.
  • 祂以能力平静大海，祂藉聪明打伤拉哈伯；
  • 26:13　By His Spirit the heavens became beauty; His hand pierced the fleeing serpent.
  • 藉祂的气使天晴朗；祂的手刺透快逃的蛇。
  • 26:14　Indeed, these are but the fringes of His ways; And how small a whisper do we hear of Him! But as for the thundering of His mightiness, who can comprehend it?
  • 看哪，这些不过是神作为的些微；我们所听于祂的，是何等细微的声音！至于祂大能的雷声，谁能明了呢？
