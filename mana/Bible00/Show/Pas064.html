诗篇-Psalms 64

Settings

  • 64:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 64:1　Hear, O God, my voice in my complaint; Preserve my life from the enemy's dread.
  • 神阿，我哀叹的时候，求你听我的声音；求你保守我的性命，不受仇敌的惊恐。
  • 64:2　Hide me from the secret counsel of evildoers, From the throng of the workers of iniquity,
  • 求你把我隐藏，使我脱离作恶之人的密谋，和作孽之人的骚乱；
  • 64:3　Who have sharpened their tongue like a sword, Who have aimed their arrow, their bitter word,
  • 他们磨舌如刀，发出苦毒的言语，好像比准了的箭，
  • 64:4　To shoot in secret places at the perfect man. They shoot suddenly at him and do not fear.
  • 要在暗处射完全人。他们忽然射他，并不惧怕。
  • 64:5　They firm up for themselves an evil matter; They talk of laying snares secretly; They say, Who will see them?
  • 他们彼此勉励设计恶事；他们商量暗设网罗，说，谁能看见？
  • 64:6　They seek out injustices, saying: We have completed a carefully sought-out plan. Indeed each man's inward thought And heart are deep.
  • 他们图谋不义，说，我们完成了精密的计谋；他们各人的意念和心思是深的。
  • 64:7　But God will shoot an arrow at them; Suddenly they are wounded.
  • 但神要用箭射他们，他们就忽然受伤。
  • 64:8　They are made to stumble over each other by their own tongue; All who see them shake their heads.
  • 他们必被自己的舌头所害而绊跌；凡看见他们的，必都摇头。
  • 64:9　And all men will fear And declare what has been done by God, And they will ponder His work.
  • 众人都要害怕，要传扬神的工作，并且思考祂的作为。
  • 64:10　The righteous man will rejoice in Jehovah and take refuge in Him, And all the upright in heart will boast.
  • 义人必因耶和华喜乐，并要投靠祂；凡心里正直的人，都要夸口。
