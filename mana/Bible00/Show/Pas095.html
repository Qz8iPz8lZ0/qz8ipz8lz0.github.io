诗篇-Psalms 95

Settings

  • 95:1　Come, let us sing for joy to Jehovah; Let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.
  • 来阿，我们要向耶和华欢唱，向拯救我们的磐石欢呼。
  • 95:2　Let us come before His presence with thanksgiving; Let us make a joyful noise to Him with psalms.
  • 我们要到祂面前来称谢祂，用诗歌向祂欢呼。
  • 95:3　For Jehovah is a great God And a great King above all gods.
  • 因耶和华是大神，是大君王，超乎万神之上。
  • 95:4　In His hand are the depths of the earth, The heights of the mountains are His also.
  • 地的深处在祂手中，山的高峰也属祂。
  • 95:5　The sea is His, and He made it; And His hands formed the dry land.
  • 海洋属祂，是祂造的；旱地也是祂手造成的。
  • 95:6　Come, let us worship and bow down; Let us kneel before Jehovah our Maker.
  • 来阿，我们要屈身敬拜，在造我们的耶和华面前跪下。
  • 95:7　For He is our God, And we are the people of His pasture And the flock of His hand. Today, if you hear His voice,
  • 因为祂是我们的神，我们是祂草场上的民，是祂手下的羊。你们今日若听见祂的声音，
  • 95:8　Do not harden your heart as at Meribah, As in the day of Massah in the wilderness;
  • 就不可硬着心，像在米利巴，像在旷野玛撒的日子一样；
  • 95:9　When your fathers tested Me; They tried Me, even though they had seen My work.
  • 那时你们的祖宗虽然看见我的作为，还是试探我，试验我。
  • 95:10　For forty years I loathed that generation, And I said, They are a people who go astray in heart; And they do not know My ways;
  • 四十年之久，我厌烦那一代的人，说，他们是心里迷糊的百姓，竟不晓得我的法则；
  • 95:11　Therefore I swore in My anger: They shall by no means enter into My rest!
  • 所以我在怒中起誓，说，他们绝不可进入我的安息！
