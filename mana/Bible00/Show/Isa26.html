以赛亚书-Isaiah 26

Settings

  • 26:1　In that day this song will be sung in the land of Judah: We have a strong city; He will set up salvation as walls and a rampart.
  • 当那日，在犹大地人必唱这歌，说，我们有坚固的城；耶和华要将救恩立为城墙和外郭。
  • 26:2　Open the gates So that the righteous nation may enter, They who maintain faithfulness.
  • 当敞开城门，使持守忠信的公义国民得以进入。
  • 26:3　You will keep the steadfast of mind In perfect peace Because he trusts in You.
  • 心意坚定的，你必保守他十分平安，因为他信靠你。
  • 26:4　Trust in Jehovah forever and ever, For in Jah Jehovah we have an eternal rock.
  • 你们当信靠耶和华，直到永远；因为在主耶和华里，我们有永久的磐石。
  • 26:5　For He has brought down those who dwell on high, The lofty city; He lays it low, He lays it low to the ground; He casts it into the dust.
  • 祂使住在高处的，与高城一并倾倒，将城拆毁，拆平直到尘埃。
  • 26:6　The foot will trample it, the foot of the poor, The steps of the weak.
  • 它要被脚践踏，就是被困苦人的脚和穷乏人的脚践踏。
  • 26:7　The path of the righteous is smoothness; O You who are upright, You make the path of the righteous level.
  • 义人的道路是平直的；正直的主阿，你必修平义人的路径。
  • 26:8　Indeed in the path of Your judgments, O Jehovah, we have waited for You. Your name, that is, Your memorial, Is the desire of our soul.
  • 耶和华阿，我们在你行审判的路上等候你。我们魂里所羡慕的是你的名，就是你那可记念的名。
  • 26:9　My soul desires You in the night; Indeed my spirit within me seeks You at the dawn; For when Your judgments are in the earth, The inhabitants of the world learn righteousness.
  • 夜间我的魂羡慕你；清晨我里面的灵寻求你；因为你在地上行审判的时候，世上的居民就学习公义。
  • 26:10　Though the wicked is shown grace, He does not learn righteousness; In the land of straightforwardness he deals unjustly And does not perceive the majesty of Jehovah.
  • 恶人虽得恩惠，仍不学习公义；在正直的地上，他必行事不义，也不注意耶和华的威严。
  • 26:11　O Jehovah, Your hand is lifted up, But they do not see it. They will see Your zeal for the people and will be put to shame; Indeed fire will devour Your adversaries.
  • 耶和华阿，你的手高举，他们仍看不见。他们必看见你为百姓发的热心，因而抱愧；并且有火要烧灭你的敌人。
  • 26:12　O Jehovah, You will ordain peace for us, For You have also performed all our works for us.
  • 耶和华阿，你必派定我们得平安，因为我们所作的事，也都是你给我们成就的。
  • 26:13　O Jehovah our God, other lords besides You have been master to us, But through You alone do we mention Your name.
  • 耶和华我们的神阿，在你以外曾有别的主管辖我们，但我们惟独靠你题说你的名。
  • 26:14　The dead will not live, The deceased will not rise; Therefore You have visited and destroyed them, And You have caused all memory of them to perish.
  • 已死的必不能再活，去世的必不能再起；所以你刑罚了他们，毁灭了他们，又使他们的名号全然消灭。
  • 26:15　You have increased the nation, O Jehovah; You have increased the nation-You are glorified; You have far extended all the edges of the land.
  • 耶和华阿，你增添国民，你增添国民，你得了荣耀；你又扩张地的四境到远处。
  • 26:16　O Jehovah, in distress they sought You; They whispered out a prayer, for Your chastening was upon them.
  • 耶和华阿，他们在急难中寻求你；你的惩罚临到他们身上，他们就低声倾吐，向你祷告。
  • 26:17　As a pregnant woman draws near to giving birth, She writhes and cries out in her pain; So we have been before You, O Jehovah.
  • 妇人怀孕，临产疼痛，在痛苦之中喊叫；耶和华阿，我们在你面前也是如此。
  • 26:18　We were pregnant; we writhed; we gave birth, as it were, to wind. We have not accomplished deliverance for the earth, Nor have the inhabitants of the world been born.
  • 我们也曾怀孕疼痛，所产的竟像风一样。我们未曾给地行什么拯救的事，世上的居民也未曾由我们而生。
  • 26:19　Your dead will live; my corpses will rise. Awake and give a ringing shout, you who dwell in dust, For your dew is like the dew of the dawn, And the earth will bring forth the deceased.
  • 属你的死人要活过来；我们的尸首要兴起。住在尘埃中的阿，要醒起欢呼，因你的甘露好像清晨的甘露，地也要交出死人来。
  • 26:20　Come, my people, enter your rooms, And shut your doors behind you; Hide for a short moment Until the indignation has passed;
  • 我的百姓阿，你们要来，进入自己的内室，关上门，隐藏片时，等到忿怒过去；
  • 26:21　For Jehovah is now coming forth from His place To punish the inhabitants of the earth for their iniquity; And the earth will reveal her bloodshed And will no longer conceal her slain.
  • 因为耶和华从祂的住处出来，要刑罚地上居民的罪孽；地也必露出其中的血，不再掩盖被杀的人。
