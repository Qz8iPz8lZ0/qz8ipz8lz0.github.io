诗篇-Psalms 48

Settings

  • 48:0
  • 一首歌，可拉子孙的诗。
  • 48:1　Great is Jehovah, And much to be praised In the city of our God, In His holy mountain.
  • 耶和华为大，在我们神的城中，在祂的圣山上，该大受赞美。
  • 48:2　Beautiful in elevation, The joy of the whole earth, Is Mount Zion, the sides of the north, The city of the great King.
  • 在北面的锡安山，是大君王的城，居高华美，为全地所喜悦。
  • 48:3　God has made Himself known In her palaces as a high retreat.
  • 神在城的宫中自显为高台。
  • 48:4　For, behold, the kings have assembled themselves; They passed by together.
  • 看哪，众王会合，一同经过。
  • 48:5　They saw; therefore they were astounded. They were dismayed; they hurried off in alarm.
  • 他们见了这城，就诧异惊惶，急忙逃跑。
  • 48:6　Trembling seized them there; Writhing, like that of a woman in labor;
  • 战兢在那里抓住他们；他们疼痛挣扎，好像产难的妇人一样。
  • 48:7　With an east wind You shattered The ships of Tarshish.
  • 神阿，你用东风打破他施的船只。
  • 48:8　As we have heard, So we have seen In the city of Jehovah of hosts, In the city of our God. God will establish her forever.
  • 我们在万军之耶和华的城中，就是我们神的城中，所看见的，正如我们所听见的。神必坚立这城，直到永远。　细拉
  • 48:9　We consider, O God, Your lovingkindness In the midst of Your temple.
  • 神阿，我们在你的殿中，想念你的慈爱。
  • 48:10　As Your name is, O God, So is Your praise Unto the ends of the earth; Your right hand is full of righteousness.
  • 神阿，你受的赞美，正如你的名一样，直到地极；你的右手满了公义。
  • 48:11　Let Mount Zion rejoice; Let the daughters of Judah exult Because of Your judgments.
  • 因你的判断，锡安山应当快乐，犹大的女子应当欢腾。
  • 48:12　Walk about Zion, and go around her; Count her towers.
  • 你们当周游锡安，四围旋绕，数点城楼。
  • 48:13　Consider carefully her bulwarks; Pass between her palaces; That you may recount it to the generation following.
  • 当细看她的外郭，巡察她的宫殿，为要传述给后代。
  • 48:14　For this God is our God forever and ever. He will guide us even unto death.
  • 因为这神永永远远为我们的神；祂必引导我们，直到死时。
