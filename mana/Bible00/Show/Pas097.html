诗篇-Psalms 97

Settings

  • 97:1　Jehovah reigns! Let the earth be glad; Let the many islands rejoice.
  • 耶和华作王！愿地欢腾，愿众海岛喜乐。
  • 97:2　Clouds and deep darkness surround Him; Righteousness and justice are the foundation of His throne.
  • 密云和幽暗，在祂的四围；公义和公平，是祂宝座的根基。
  • 97:3　A fire goes before Him And burns up His adversaries all around.
  • 有火在祂前头行，烧灭祂四围的敌人。
  • 97:4　His lightning illumines the world; The earth sees and trembles.
  • 祂的闪电光照世界；地看见便震动。
  • 97:5　The mountains melt like wax at the presence of Jehovah, At the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.
  • 诸山见耶和华的面，就是全地之主的面，便镕化如蜡。
  • 97:6　The heavens declare His righteousness, And all the peoples see His glory.
  • 诸天传扬祂的公义，万民看见祂的荣耀。
  • 97:7　Let all who serve a graven image be ashamed, Who make their boast in idols; Worship Him, all you gods.
  • 愿一切事奉雕制的偶像，靠虚无之神像自夸的，都蒙羞愧；万神哪，你们都当拜祂。
  • 97:8　Zion hears and rejoices, And the daughters of Judah are glad, Because of Your judgments, O Jehovah.
  • 耶和华阿，因你的判断，锡安听见就喜乐，犹大的女子也欢腾。
  • 97:9　For You, O Jehovah, are most high over all the earth; You are exalted far above all gods.
  • 因为耶和华阿，你是管理全地的至高者；你被尊崇，远超万神之上。
  • 97:10　O you who love Jehovah, hate evil! He preserves the souls of His faithful ones; He delivers them from the hand of the wicked.
  • 你们爱耶和华的，当恨恶邪恶！祂保护虔诚人的性命，搭救他们脱离恶人的手。
  • 97:11　Light is sown for the righteous man, And rejoicing for the upright in heart.
  • 撒播亮光是为义人，撒播喜乐是为心里正直的人。
  • 97:12　Rejoice in Jehovah, O righteous ones; And praise His holy memorial.
  • 义人哪，你们当因耶和华喜乐，赞美祂可记念的圣名。
