诗篇-Psalms 96

Settings

  • 96:1　Sing to Jehovah a new song; Sing to Jehovah, all the earth.
  • 你们要向耶和华唱新歌；全地都要向耶和华歌唱。
  • 96:2　Sing to Jehovah, bless His name; Proclaim His salvation from day to day.
  • 要向耶和华歌唱，颂赞祂的名，天天传扬祂的救恩。
  • 96:3　Declare His glory among the nations, His wondrous deeds among all the peoples.
  • 要在列邦中述说祂的荣耀，在万民中述说祂的奇事。
  • 96:4　For Jehovah is great and much to be praised; He is to be feared above all gods.
  • 因耶和华为大，当受极大的赞美；祂在万神之上当受敬畏。
  • 96:5　For all the gods of the peoples are idols, But Jehovah made the heavens.
  • 众民的神都属虚无，惟独耶和华造作诸天。
  • 96:6　Majesty and splendor are before Him; Strength and beauty are in His sanctuary.
  • 有尊荣和威严在祂面前，有能力与华美在祂圣所。
  • 96:7　Ascribe to Jehovah, O families of the peoples, Ascribe to Jehovah, glory and strength;
  • 众民中的各族阿，你们要将荣耀能力归给耶和华，都归给耶和华；
  • 96:8　Ascribe to Jehovah the glory of His name; Bring an offering, and come into His courts.
  • 要将耶和华的名所当得的荣耀归给祂，拿供物来进入祂的院宇。
  • 96:9　Worship Jehovah in holy splendor; Tremble before Him, all the earth.
  • 当以圣别的妆饰敬拜耶和华；全地要在祂面前战抖。
  • 96:10　Say among the nations, Jehovah reigns; Indeed the world has been established; it will not be moved; He will judge the peoples with equity.
  • 要在列邦中说，耶和华作王；世界就坚立，必不动摇；祂要按公正审判众民。
  • 96:11　Let the heavens rejoice and the earth be glad; Let the sea and its fullness roar.
  • 愿天喜乐，愿地欢腾；愿海和其中所充满的澎湃；
  • 96:12　Let the field and all that is in it exult; Then all the trees of the forest will sing for joy
  • 愿田和其中所有的都欢乐；那时，林中的树木都要在耶和华面前欢唱，
  • 96:13　Before Jehovah, for He is coming; For He is coming to judge the earth; He will judge the world with righteousness, And the peoples with His truth.
  • 因为祂来了；祂来要审判这地；祂要按公义审判世界，按祂的真实判断众民。
