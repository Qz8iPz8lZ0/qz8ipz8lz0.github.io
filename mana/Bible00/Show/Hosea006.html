何西阿书-Hosea 6

Settings

  • 6:1　Come and let us return to Jehovah; For He has torn us, but He will heal us, And He has stricken us, but He will bind us up.
  • 来罢，我们归向耶和华；祂撕裂我们，也必医治；祂打伤我们，也必缠裹。
  • 6:2　He will enliven us after two days; On the third day He will raise us up, And we will live in His presence.
  • 过两天祂必使我们活过来，第三天祂必使我们兴起，我们就在祂面前活着。
  • 6:3　Therefore let us know, let us pursue knowing Jehovah: His going forth is as sure as the dawn, And He will come to us as the rain, As the latter rain which waters the earth.
  • 我们务要认识耶和华，竭力追求认识祂；祂出现确定如晨光，祂必临到我们像甘雨，像滋润大地的春雨。
  • 6:4　What shall I do with you, O Ephraim? What shall I do with you, O Judah? For your lovingkindness is like a morning cloud And like dew that departs early.
  • 主说，以法莲哪，我可向你怎样行呢？犹大阿，我可向你怎样作呢？因为你们的慈爱如同早晨的云雾，又如速散的甘露。
  • 6:5　Therefore I hewed them to pieces by the prophets; I slew them by the words of My mouth, And My judgments went forth as light.
  • 因此，我藉申言者砍伐他们，以我口中的话杀戮他们，我的审判如光发出。
  • 6:6　For I delight in lovingkindness and not sacrifice, And the knowledge of God more than burnt offerings.
  • 我喜悦慈爱，不喜悦祭祀，喜悦认识神，胜于燔祭。
  • 6:7　But they like Adam have transgressed the covenant; There they have acted treacherously against Me.
  • 他们却如亚当背约，在那里向我行事诡诈。
  • 6:8　Gilead is a city of those who commit iniquity; It is tracked with blood.
  • 基列是行罪孽之人的城，被血沾染。
  • 6:9　And like bands of robbers lying in wait for a man, The company of priests murder on the way to Shechem; Indeed they commit wickedness.
  • 强盗成群，怎样埋伏杀人；祭司结党，也照样在示剑的路上杀戮行邪恶。
  • 6:10　In the house of Israel I have seen a horrid thing: The harlotry of Ephraim is there; Israel has defiled itself.
  • 在以色列家，我见了可怕的事：以法莲在那里有淫行；以色列玷污了自己。
  • 6:11　Also, O Judah, there is a harvest appointed for you, When I will turn the captivity of My people.
  • 犹大阿，我使被掳之民归回的时候，必有为你所命定的收成。
