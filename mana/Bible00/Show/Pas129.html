诗篇-Psalms 129

Settings

  • 129:0
  • 上行之歌。
  • 129:1　Many times have they afflicted me since my youth, Oh, let Israel say,
  • 以色列当说，从我幼年以来，敌人屡次苦害我，
  • 129:2　Many times have they afflicted me since my youth, Yet they did not prevail against me.
  • 从我幼年以来，敌人屡次苦害我，却没有胜过我。
  • 129:3　The plowers plowed on my back; They made their furrows long.
  • 他们好像扶犁的，在我背上扶犁而耕；耕的犁沟甚长。
  • 129:4　Jehovah is righteous; He has cut the cords of the wicked.
  • 耶和华是公义的；祂砍断了恶人的绳索。
  • 129:5　May all who hate Zion Be put to shame and turned back.
  • 愿恨恶锡安的，都蒙羞退后。
  • 129:6　May they be like grass on the housetops, Which withers before it grows up,
  • 愿他们像房顶上的草，未长成而枯萎；
  • 129:7　With which the reaper does not fill his hand, Or he who binds sheaves, his bosom.
  • 收割的不够一把，捆禾的也不够满怀。
  • 129:8　Nor may those who pass by say, The blessing of Jehovah be upon you! We bless you in the name of Jehovah.
  • 过路的也不说，愿耶和华所赐的福，归于你们；我们在耶和华的名里，给你们祝福。
