诗篇-Psalms 21

Settings

  • 21:0
  • 大卫的诗，交与歌咏长。
  • 21:1　O Jehovah, in Your strength the king rejoices, And in Your salvation how greatly does he exult.
  • 耶和华阿，王因你的能力喜乐；他因你的救恩，何其欢腾。
  • 21:2　You have given him his heart's desire And have not withheld the request of his lips.
  • 他心里所愿的，你已经赐给他；他嘴唇所求的，你未尝不应允。　细拉
  • 21:3　For You meet him with blessings of goodness; You have set a crown of refined gold on his head.
  • 你以美福迎接他，把精金的冠冕戴在他头上。
  • 21:4　He asked life of You; You granted it to him: Length of days forever and ever.
  • 他向你求寿，你便赐给他，就是日子长久，直到永远。
  • 21:5　His glory is great in Your salvation; You have laid majesty and splendor upon him;
  • 他因你的救恩大有荣耀；你将尊荣威严加在他身上；
  • 21:6　For You have made him to be blessings forever; You have made him glad with rejoicing in Your presence.
  • 你使他有洪福，直到永远；又使他在你面前欢喜快乐。
  • 21:7　For the king trusts in Jehovah; And because of the lovingkindness of the Most High, he is not shaken.
  • 王信靠耶和华；因至高者的慈爱，他必不摇动。
  • 21:8　Your hand finds all Your enemies; Your right hand finds those who hate You.
  • 你的手要搜出你的一切仇敌；你的右手要搜出那些恨你的人。
  • 21:9　You make them like a furnace of fire When Your presence appears. Jehovah swallows them in His anger, And fire devours them.
  • 你面光显现的时候，要使他们如在炎热的火炉中；耶和华要在祂的怒中吞灭他们，火要把他们烧尽了。
  • 21:10　You destroy their fruit from the earth, And their seed from among the sons of men.
  • 你必从地上灭绝他们的子孙，从世人中间灭绝他们的后裔。
  • 21:11　Although they intend evil against You, And they think up a scheme, they will not prevail.
  • 因为他们有意加害于你；他们想出计谋，却不能成功。
  • 21:12　For You make them turn their back; You aim with Your bowstrings at their faces.
  • 你必使他们转背逃跑；你对准他们的脸，搭箭在弦。
  • 21:13　Be exalted, O Jehovah, in Your strength; We will sing and psalm Your might.
  • 耶和华阿，愿你因自己的能力得高举；我们就唱诗，歌颂你的大能。
