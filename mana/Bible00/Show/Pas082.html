诗篇-Psalms 82

Settings

  • 82:0
  • 亚萨的诗。
  • 82:1　God stands in the congregation of God; He judges in the midst of the gods.
  • 神站在神的会中；在诸神中行审判，
  • 82:2　How long will you judge unjustly And respect the persons of the wicked?
  • 说，你们审判不秉公义，徇恶人的情面，要到几时呢？　细拉
  • 82:3　Judge the poor and the orphan; Execute justice for the afflicted and the destitute;
  • 你们当为贫寒的人和孤儿伸冤，为困苦和贫穷的人施行公理；
  • 82:4　Rescue the poor and the needy; Deliver them from the hand of the wicked.
  • 当救护贫寒和穷乏的人，搭救他们脱离恶人的手。
  • 82:5　You do not know, nor do you understand; You go about in darkness; All the foundations of the earth are shaken.
  • 你们仍不知道，也不明白，在黑暗中走来走去；地的根基都摇动了。
  • 82:6　I said, You are gods, And all of you are sons of the Most High.
  • 我曾说，你们是神，都是至高者的儿子。
  • 82:7　Nevertheless you will die like men And fall like one of the princes.
  • 然而，你们要死，与众人一样；要仆倒，像首领中的一位。
  • 82:8　Arise, O God; judge the earth; For You will inherit all the nations.
  • 神阿，求你起来，审判这地；因为你要得万邦为业。
