诗篇-Psalms 16

Settings

  • 16:0
  • 大卫的金诗。
  • 16:1　Preserve me, O God, for I take refuge in You.
  • 神阿，求你保守我，因为我投奔于你。
  • 16:2　I say to Jehovah, You are my Lord; No good have I beyond You;
  • 我对耶和华说，你是我的主；我的好处不在你以外。
  • 16:3　As for the saints who are on the earth, they are the excellent; All my delight is in them.
  • 论到地上的圣民，他们是尊高的人，是我所最喜悦的。
  • 16:4　The sorrows of them who bartered for some other god will be multiplied; Their drink offerings of blood I will not offer, Nor will I take up their names upon my lips.
  • 以别神代替耶和华的，他们的愁苦必加增；他们所浇奠的血我不献上，我嘴唇也不题别神的名号。
  • 16:5　Jehovah is the portion of my inheritance and of my cup; You maintain my lot.
  • 耶和华是我的产业，是我杯中的分；我所得的分你为我持守。
  • 16:6　The measuring lines have fallen on pleasant places for me; Indeed the inheritance is beautiful to me.
  • 用绳量给我的地界，坐落在佳美之处；我的产业实在美好。
  • 16:7　I will bless Jehovah, who counsels me; Indeed in the nights my inward parts instruct me.
  • 我必颂赞那指教我的耶和华；我的心肠在夜间也警戒我。
  • 16:8　I have set Jehovah before me continually; Because He is at my right hand, I shall not be shaken.
  • 我将耶和华常摆在我面前；因祂在我右边，我便不至摇动。
  • 16:9　Therefore my heart rejoices and my glory exults; Even my flesh dwells securely.
  • 因此我的心快乐，我的灵欢腾；我的肉身也安然居住。
  • 16:10　For You will not abandon my soul to Sheol, Nor let Your Holy One see the pit.
  • 因为你必不将我的魂撇在阴间，也不叫你的圣者见朽坏。
  • 16:11　You will make known to me the path of life; In Your presence is fullness of joy; At Your right hand there are pleasures forever.
  • 你必将生命的道路指示我；在你面前有满足的喜乐；在你右手中有永远的福乐。
