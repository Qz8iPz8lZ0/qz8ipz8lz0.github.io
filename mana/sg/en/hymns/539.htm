Chorus line 3: 'admire' changed to 'enjoy'

 

 1  O Lord, Thou art in me as life

       And everything to me!

    Subjective and available,

       Thus I experience Thee.

 

O Lord, Thou art the Spirit!

   How dear and near to me!

How I enjoy Thy marvelous

   Availability!

 

 2  To all my needs both great and small

       Thou art the rich supply;

    So ready and sufficient too

       For me now to apply.

 

O Lord, Thou art the Spirit!

   How dear and near to me!

How I enjoy Thy marvelous

   Availability!

 

 3  Thy sweet anointing with Thy might

       In weakness doth sustain;

    By Thy supply of energy

       My strength Thou dost maintain.

 

O Lord, Thou art the Spirit!

   How dear and near to me!

How I enjoy Thy marvelous

   Availability!

 

 4  Thy law of life in heart and mind

       My conduct regulates;

    The wealth of Thy reality

       My being saturates.

 

O Lord, Thou art the Spirit!

   How dear and near to me!

How I enjoy Thy marvelous

   Availability!

 

 5  O Thou art ever one with me,

       Unrivaled unity!

    One spirit with me all the time

       For all eternity!

 

O Lord, Thou art the Spirit!

   How dear and near to me!

How I enjoy Thy marvelous

   Availability!
