Comfort in Trials - By the Lord's Care

Eng:700  Chin:-  Kor:-  Span:-  Tag:700  Fra:-  Por:- 

Lyrics:J. H. S.
Music:Arthur S. Sullivan
Meter:8.3.8.3.8.7

This hymn has a unique tune

Home | Browse | Prev | Next

Verses:  1   2 

 

 1  "Be not anxious for the morrow,"

          Jesus said;

    Why should we its trials borrow?

          Trust instead!

    For each hour of need or sorrow,

       Grace He gives, and daily bread.

 

 2  Thus our treasures safe investing

          In His care,

    Kept in His pavilion resting,

          May we dare

    Still to trust, thru faith's deep testing,

       Sure, God will His ways declare.
