Longings - For Likeness to Christ

Eng:400  Chin:-  Kor:-  Span:-  Tag:400  Fra:-  Por:- 

Lyrics:James Rowe
Music:Bentley D. Ackley
Meter:8.6.8.6. with chorus

This hymn has a unique tune

Home | Browse | Prev | Next

Verses:  1   2   3 

 

 1  Earthly pleasures vainly call me,

       I would be like Jesus;

    Nothing worldly shall enthrall me,

       I would be like Jesus.

 

Be like Jesus, this my song,

   In the home and in the throng;

Be like Jesus, all day long!

   I would be like Jesus.

 

 2  He has broken every fetter,

       I would be like Jesus;

    That my soul may serve Him better,

       I would be like Jesus.

 

Be like Jesus, this my song,

   In the home and in the throng;

Be like Jesus, all day long!

   I would be like Jesus.

 

 3  All the way from earth to glory,

       I would be like Jesus;

    Telling o'er and o'er the story,

       I would be like Jesus.

 

Be like Jesus, this my song,

   In the home and in the throng;

Be like Jesus, all day long!

   I would be like Jesus.
