Hymns, #449

Consecration - Yielding to the Lord

Eng:449  Chin:344  Kor:344  Span:193  Tag:449  Fra:-  Por:206 

Lyrics:Adelaide A. Pollard
Music:George C. Stebbins
Meter:5.4.5.4.D

This hymn has a unique tune

Verses:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1  Have Thine own way, Lord,

       Have Thine own way;

    Thou art the Potter,

       I am the clay.

    Mould me and make me

       After Thy will,

    While I am waiting,

       Yielded and still.

 

 2  Have Thine own way, Lord,

       Have Thine own way;

    Search me and try me,

       Master, today.

    Whiter than snow, Lord,

       Wash me just now,

    As in Thy presence

       Humbly I bow.

 

 3  Have Thine own way, Lord,

       Have Thine own way;

    Wounded and weary,

       Help me, I pray.

    Power, all power,

       Surely is Thine,

    Touch me and heal me,

       Savior divine.

 

 4  Have Thine own way, Lord,

       Have Thine own way;

    Hold o'er my being

       Absolute sway.

    Fill with Thy Spirit

       Till all shall see

    Christ only, always,

       Living in me.
