Verses:  1   2   3 

 

 1  Our God is living — say, Hallelujah!

    He's living in us — say, Hallelujah!

       We taste His riches,

       He is our portion.

    Just say 'O Lord' to taste and see.

 

 2  We come together — there's nothing better —

    For in the Spirit we are His family;

       His living Body!

       We just express Him.

    Jesus is our reality.

 

 3  In all the churches we're being filled now

    For the saturation throughout the nation,

       To spread our Jesus

       To every city —

    This land will see the living Christ.
