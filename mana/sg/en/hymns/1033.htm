Gospel - Persuasion

Lyrics:Haldor Lillenas
Music:Haldor Lillenas
Meter:10.6.10.6. with chorus

Verses:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1  Ye who are troubled and burdened by sin,

       Come just as you are;

    Come to the Savior, a new life begin,

       Oh, come just as you are.

 

Come just as you are,

   Oh, come just as you are;

Give up your sin, let the Savior come in,

   And come just as you are.

 

 2  Deep in your heart sin has written its scar,

       Come just as you are;

    Though from the Father you've wandered afar,

       Oh, come just as you are.

 

Come just as you are,

   Oh, come just as you are;

Give up your sin, let the Savior come in,

   And come just as you are.

 

 3  Sinful and guilty, heart broken and lost,

       Come just as you are;

    Think what your ransom on Calvary cost!

       Oh, come just as you are.

 

Come just as you are,

   Oh, come just as you are;

Give up your sin, let the Savior come in,

   And come just as you are.

 

 4  Naught of your goodness God's favor can win,

       Come just as you are;

    Trust in the blood which was shed for your sin,

       And come just as you are.

 

Come just as you are,

   Oh, come just as you are;

Give up your sin, let the Savior come in,

   And come just as you are.

 

 5  Come with your heartache, your sorrow and pain,

       Come just as you are;

    No one has come to the Savior in vain,

       Oh, come just as you are.

 

Come just as you are,

   Oh, come just as you are;

Give up your sin, let the Savior come in,

   And come just as you are.
