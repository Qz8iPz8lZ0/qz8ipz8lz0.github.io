Hymns, #343

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - General

Eng:343  Chin:262  Kor:262  Span:-  Tag:343  Fra:-  Por:- 

Lyrics:Eliza E. Hewitt
Music:John R. Sweney
Meter:9.6.8.6. with chorus

This hymn has a unique tune

Verses:  1   2   3   4 

 

 1  There is sunshine in my soul today,

       More glorious and bright

    Than glows in any earthly sky,

       For Jesus is my light.

 

O there's sunshine, blessed sunshine,

While the peaceful, happy moments roll;

When Jesus shows His smiling face

There is sunshine in my soul.

 

 2  There is music in my soul today,

       A carol to my King;

    And Jesus, listening, can hear

       The song I cannot sing.

 

O there's sunshine, blessed sunshine,

While the peaceful, happy moments roll;

When Jesus shows His smiling face

There is sunshine in my soul.

 

 3  There is springtime in my soul today,

       For when the Lord is near

    The dove of peace sings in my heart,

       The flowers of grace appear.

 

O there's sunshine, blessed sunshine,

While the peaceful, happy moments roll;

When Jesus shows His smiling face

There is sunshine in my soul.

 

 4  There is gladness in my soul today,

       And hope, and praise, and love,

    For blessings which He gives me now,

       For joys laid up above.

 

O there's sunshine, blessed sunshine,

While the peaceful, happy moments roll;

When Jesus shows His smiling face

There is sunshine in my soul.
