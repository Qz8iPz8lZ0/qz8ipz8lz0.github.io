Hymns, #858

The Church - Her Fellowship

Eng:858  Chin:-  Kor:-  Span:-  Tag:-  Fra:-  Por:- 

Lyrics:Charles Wesley
Music:Thomas Hastings
Meter:8.6.8.6. with repeat

Hymns using same tune:

#177, #264, #600, #1241

Home | Browse | Prev | Next

Verses:  1   2   3   4   5 

 

 1  All praise to our redeeming Lord,

       Who joins us by His grace,

    And bids us, each to each restored,

       Together seek His face,

       Together seek His face.

 

 2  He bids us build each other up;

       And, gathered into one,

    To our high calling's glorious hope,

       We hand in hand go on,

       We hand in hand go on.

 

 3  The gift which He on one bestows,

       We all delight to prove;

    The grace through every vessel flows,

       In purest streams of love,

       In purest streams of love.

 

 4  We all partake the joy of one;

       The common peace we feel;

    A peace to worldly minds unknown,

       A joy unspeakable,

       A joy unspeakable.

 

 5  And if our fellowship below

       In Christ yet be so sweet,

    What height of rapture shall we know,

       When round His throne we meet,

       When round His throne we meet!
