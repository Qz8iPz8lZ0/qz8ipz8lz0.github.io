Hymns, #306

Assurance and Joy of Salvation - Redeemed by the Blood

Eng:306  Chin:-  Kor:-  Span:149  Tag:306  Fra:306  Por:- 

Lyrics:M. Fraser
Music:M. A. Sea
Meter:6.5.7.5

This hymn has a unique tune

Verses:  1   2   3   4   5   6   7 

 

 1     I belong to Jesus;

       I am not my own;

    All I have and all I am

       Shall be His alone.

 

 2     I belong to Jesus;

       He is Lord and King,

    Reigning in my inmost heart

       Over everything.

 

 3     I belong to Jesus;

       What can hurt or harm,

    When He folds around my soul

       His almighty Arm?

 

 4     I belong to Jesus;

       Blessed, blessed thought!

    With His own most precious blood

       Has my soul been bought.

 

 5     I belong to Jesus;

       He has died for me;

    I am His and He is mine

       Through eternity.

 

 6     I belong to Jesus;

       He will keep my soul,

    If the deathly waters dark

       Round about me roll.

 

 7     I belong to Jesus;

       And ere long I'll be

    With my precious Savior there

       In His royalty.
